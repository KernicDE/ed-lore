A health-conscious video recorded by holovid action star William “Big Bill” Ventura has gone viral on interstellar networks, sparking a wave of positivity.
Known for comedic roles in traditional ‘real cast’ productions such as ‘Malacticus Runs Again’ and ‘For A Few Credits Less’, Ventura’s notoriously vibrant party lifestyle has often made him a popular figure of fun and mischief in many celebrity gossip sections.
But a recent self-produced video of Ventura’s workout regimen has exploded in popularity, with the actor showing a remarkable body transformation to lean, fit and strong.
“I’ve dedicated the past year to improving myself after growing unhappy with my image,” Ventura told Corinne Macintyre of the Alliance Tribune. “My energy levels have been through the roof, and after putting in so much effort I feel like I can achieve anything! I want to hike across the rainforests of Planet Lave in support of ongoing conservation efforts there.”
Messages of support and clips of people following Ventura’s workouts have poured in, with millions of responses attributed to the original video. Ventura called the response ‘mind-blowing, just crazy to see so much love and healthy choices resulting from all this.’
