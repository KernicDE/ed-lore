Pirate POWs Pack Prisons

The Pegasi Pirate War stemming from the United Imperial Offensive has taxed the logistical prowess of the Empire. With many thousands of prisoners now in custody, accommodation is becoming an issue. Prisoners are currently held in hundreds of locations, creating additional logistical problems. Sources within Empire have indicated that a search for a suitable penal station, capable of holding all the prisoners, has begun.
