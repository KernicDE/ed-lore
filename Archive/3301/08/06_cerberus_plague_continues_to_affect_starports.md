Cerberus Plague Continues to Affect Starports

Although a cure for the Cerberus Plague has been found, the disease has not yet been eradicated, and it continues to wreak havoc in certain systems.

The following systems have been affected by the plague: Cantjarisni, Rajukru, HIP 10175, Amahu, Leerham, BD-11 4280, Louguala.

In these systems, the following station services are unavailable: Re-arming, Market, Bulletin board, Docking, Shipyard, Outfitting, Black market.
