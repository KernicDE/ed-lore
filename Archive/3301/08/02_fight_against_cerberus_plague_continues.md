Fight Against Cerberus Plague Continues

Medical teams across the galaxy continue their efforts to distribute the Cerberus Plague antidote to affected systems. Despite the discovery of the cure, the disease continues to claim lives in systems that have not yet received sufficient supplies.

"We are working as fast as we can," said Chief Medical Officer Carl Simmons. "But the scale of this outbreak is unprecedented. We need more ships, more pilots, and more time."
