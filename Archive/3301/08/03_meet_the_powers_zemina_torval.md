Meet the Powers – Zemina Torval

In this eighth article of the series, Marcus Macmillan examines Senator Zemina Torval.

Senator Torval is one of the most powerful and controversial figures in the Empire. A staunch defender of Imperial tradition, she is the owner of the Imperial Slavers Association and one of the richest individuals in the Empire.

"Imperial Slavery is not barbarism," Torval insists. "It is a safety net. It ensures that no one starves, no one falls into poverty. Those who criticise it do not understand its true purpose."

Her feud with Aisling Duval over the issue of slavery has become one of the defining political conflicts of 3301. While Aisling appeals to the younger generation's idealism, Torval speaks to those who value tradition and stability.
