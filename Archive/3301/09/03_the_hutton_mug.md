The Hutton Mug

The words "I made it to Hutton Orbital" carry a certain amount of weight among pilots. As seasoned navigators know, the starport lies approximately 0.22 light years from the nearest star – a significant stretch by anyone's standards.

Now, a company called BlipMagnet has decided to capitalise on this phenomenon with the Hutton mug, a commemorative beaker bearing the legend 'I made it to Hutton Orbital'. The novelty item is available only at the starport.

BlipMagnet claims (presumably only half seriously) that the mug is made from "the salvaged frame shift drive plates of ships that ran out of fuel before reaching the station".
