Merchant Marines Launch Operation Papercut

The Merchant Marines have announced the launch of Operation Papercut, a coordinated effort to protect trade routes in the Old Worlds region from pirate activity.

Commanders interested in signing up for the operation should report to their nearest Merchant Marines liaison office.
