Reorte Liberated by the Alliance

The Alliance has announced the successful liberation of Reorte from pirate control. The operation, carried out by Alliance forces with support from independent pilots, has restored order to the system.

The Reorte Free State has pledged its support to the Alliance, strengthening the superpower's presence in the region.
