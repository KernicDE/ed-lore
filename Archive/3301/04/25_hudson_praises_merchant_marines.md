Hudson Praises Merchant Marines

Shadow President Hudson has publicly praised the Merchant Marines for their recent efforts in protecting Federal trade interests. In a statement released from his office on Mars, Hudson said:

"The Merchant Marines represent the best of what the Federation has to offer. Their dedication to protecting our economic interests is commendable, and I fully support their ongoing operations."
