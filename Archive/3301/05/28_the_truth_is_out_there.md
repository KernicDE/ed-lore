The Truth is Out There

As the search for Spaceflight One continues, conspiracy theories are beginning to emerge about the President's disappearance.

Dr. Arcanonn has issued a statement suggesting that the Unknown Artefacts may be connected to the incident. "We know these objects emit radiation that damages ship systems. We cannot rule out any possibility at this stage."

The Canonn have gone further, claiming that several ships experienced mysterious malfunctions near Azaleach in the days before the President's disappearance.

"The truth is out there," their spokesperson said. "And we intend to find it."
