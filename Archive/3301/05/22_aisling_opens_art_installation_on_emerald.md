Aisling Opens Art Installation on Emerald

The People's Princess made an appearance in Cemiess late last night to attend the opening of Emerald's newest art installation, 'Prism's Shades'.

Aisling dazzled crowds by dressing in a long-sleeved ruby red evening gown, adorned with painite sparkles. She left the show long before midnight, citing exhaustion from her recent activities in the Summerlands.
