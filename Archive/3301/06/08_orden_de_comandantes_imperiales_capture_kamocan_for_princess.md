Orden de Comandantes Imperiales Capture Kamocan for Princess

The Orden de Comandantes Imperiales has announced the capture of Kamocan on behalf of Princess Arissa Lavigny-Duval.

"We act in the name of the Princess and the Emperor," the group's leader declared. "Kamocan will be a beacon of Imperial justice."

The capture represents a significant expansion of Arissa's sphere of influence within the Empire.
