Simguru Offers to Share Tomorrow Today

Simguru Pranav Antal has announced that Utopia will begin sharing some of its technological advances with the wider galaxy.

"We do not seek to hoard knowledge. We seek to uplift humanity. Those who wish to learn from us are welcome."

The offer has been met with scepticism from the superpowers but enthusiasm from independent researchers.
