Galaxy's Most Powerful People: Simguru Pranav Antal

Pranav Antal, the Simguru of Utopia, is one of the most enigmatic figures in the galaxy.

Born into a wealthy corporate family on Earth, Antal rejected the materialism of Federal society in favour of a life dedicated to the pursuit of knowledge. He founded Utopia in the Antal system as an experiment in post-scarcity living, and over the decades it has grown from a small commune to a thriving society of millions.

"The Simguru is not a dictator," one Utopian citizen explained. "He is a guide. We follow him because we believe in his vision, not because he commands us to."

Antal's influence extends beyond Utopia's borders. His teachings on the Sim-Archive and the potential of neural-link technology have attracted followers from across the galaxy, including some within the superpowers themselves.

"I do not seek to conquer the galaxy," Antal has said. "I seek to change it, one mind at a time."
