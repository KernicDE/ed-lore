The Rising Cost of Getting High

Street prices for Onionhead have reached record highs following President Hudson's crackdown on drug trafficking.

"The prohibition is just making the cartels richer," one independent trader noted. "When you make something illegal, you make it valuable."

The Kappa Fornacis Farmers Union has reported record profits, despite — or perhaps because of — the Federal ban.
