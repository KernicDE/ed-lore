The Pilots Federation Supports Fuel Rat Initiative

The Pilots Federation has announced official support for the Fuel Rats, a volunteer organisation dedicated to rescuing stranded pilots.

"The Fuel Rats embody the best traditions of the Pilots Federation. We are proud to support their work."
