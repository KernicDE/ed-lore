Emperor's Grace Reaffirms Loyalty to Patreus

Despite being indicted for their role in the attempted assassination of Emperor Hengist Duval, Emperor's Grace has reaffirmed its loyalty to Senator Denton Patreus.

In a statement released from their headquarters in HR 706, the organisation declared: "We stand with Senator Patreus, who has always been a friend to our cause. The accusations against us are baseless, and we will prove our innocence in time."

Senator Patreus has not publicly responded to the statement, but sources close to the Senator say he is "grateful for their continued support."

Princess Arissa Lavigny-Duval's office issued a brief response: "Loyalty to a man accused of treason is itself an act of treason."
