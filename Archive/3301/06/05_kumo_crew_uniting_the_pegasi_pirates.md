Kumo Crew Uniting the Pegasi Pirates

Reports from the Pegasi Sector indicate that Archon Delaine is close to completing his unification of the region's pirate clans under the Kumo Crew banner.

"What we're seeing is unprecedented," said one independent security consultant. "Delaine isn't just raiding — he's building a state. The other pirate groups are either joining him or being destroyed."

The Kumo Crew's expansion has so far gone unchecked by the three superpowers, all of whom have their own crises to manage. But analysts warn that this neglect could prove costly.

"The Pegasi Sector is on the frontier of all three superpowers. If Delaine consolidates his power there, he'll have a base from which to strike at any of them."
