Only the Strong Survive the Frontier

President Hudson has issued a statement encouraging Federal citizens on the frontier to take up arms in defence of their homes.

"The frontier is a dangerous place. The Federal Navy cannot be everywhere at once. I call on all capable citizens to arm themselves and stand ready to defend their communities."
