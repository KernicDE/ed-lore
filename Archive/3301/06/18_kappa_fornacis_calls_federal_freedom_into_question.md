Kappa Fornacis Calls Federal Freedom into Question

The Kappa Fornacis Farmers Union has issued a scathing statement questioning the Federation's commitment to freedom.

"The Federation claims to stand for liberty, yet they bomb our farms, arrest our people, and destroy our livelihoods. What kind of freedom is that?"

The statement has resonated with many frontier systems who feel neglected by the Federal government.
