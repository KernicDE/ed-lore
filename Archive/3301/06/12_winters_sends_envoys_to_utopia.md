Winters Sends Envoys to Utopia

Former Acting President Felicia Winters has announced that she is sending a delegation of envoys to Utopia to discuss potential cooperation between the Federation and Antal's commune.

"Utopia has achieved remarkable things in the field of social welfare and education. I believe there is much we can learn from their approach."

The delegation will focus on Utopia's education and healthcare programmes, with a view to adapting some of their methods for use in Federal systems.

President Hudson has reportedly expressed disapproval of the initiative, with one source claiming he said: "We don't need dreamy idealists, we need results."
