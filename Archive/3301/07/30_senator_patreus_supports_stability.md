Senator Patreus Supports Stability

Senator Denton Patreus has issued a statement supporting the Emperor and the stability of the Empire.

"In these uncertain times, it is more important than ever that we remain united. The Empire has survived crises before, and we will survive this one. I pledge my full support to the Emperor and to the rule of law."

The statement has been welcomed by supporters of the Emperor, though some critics question Patreus's motives.
