Smuggling on the Rise in Conflict-Strewn Pegasi Sector

Reports from the Pegasi Sector indicate that smuggling has increased dramatically since the start of Operation Davy Jones. With trade routes disrupted by the Imperial blockade, criminal networks have stepped in to supply goods to isolated systems.

"The blockade is creating more problems than it's solving," one trader complained. "People need supplies, and if the Empire won't let them through legally, they'll find other ways."
