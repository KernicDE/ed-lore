Emperor's Grace Blocks People's Princess in Rishair

Emperor's Grace has blocked Aisling Duval's Angels from operating in the Rishair system, citing "security concerns."

The move, which came after Aisling's Angels attempted to set up a humanitarian aid station in the system, has been condemned by Aisling's office.

"This is an outrageous abuse of power. Emperor's Grace has already been indicted for treason. Now they are blocking legitimate humanitarian work. This is not the Empire I believe in."

Senator Patreus has denied ordering the block, but sources close to Emperor's Grace say they acted with his "tacit approval."

Aisling has appealed to Chancellor Blaine to intervene, saying: "This is an attack on the Imperial family. It cannot be allowed to stand."
