## Biography

The Federal Intelligence Agency (FIA) is the Federation's principal domestic intelligence and security service. Though less visible than the Federal Navy, it has played a central role in several major crises across the 3300s.

In 3304, the FIA joined a tri-superpower taskforce with the IISS and Alliance Interpol to dismantle the League of Reparation — a terrorist group murdering descendants of INRA personnel. Senior Agent Tanya Ramirez helped expose Rear Admiral Riri McAllister as the mastermind "Nexus." Later that year, Agent Micah Whitefield led a controversial sweep against the Far God cult, arresting hundreds of worshippers and exotheologian Dr Alfred Ulyanov. All detainees were eventually released when no evidence of Thargoid communication was found.

In 3305, Agent Rochelle Karim investigated the theft of the Lucifer Device — a 300-megaton fusion warhead — by the Children of Tothos cult. The crisis escalated when the cult seized Archambault Terminal with millions of civilians inside, forcing the FIA into delicate negotiations. The agency has shown both cooperative instincts and controversial overreach, including operations in foreign jurisdictions without approval.
