## Biography

Achilles Corporation is a major Federal megacorp and one of the galaxy's dominant suppliers of personal computing devices, long holding an effective duopoly alongside Sirius Corporation. Its product lines include the PA912 administration robot, of which over 300,000 units have been sold in the Federation alone.

In 3304, Achilles was embroiled in the spy robot scandal when its PA912 units were found to contain concealed surveillance programs installed by a blackmailed engineer on behalf of the Mars Tribune. The company responded by transmitting a remote kill-switch update and offering free replacements to affected customers. A decade later, its Achilles Aerospace subsidiary challenged Sirius Corporation's monopoly on Frame Shift Drive manufacture by announcing a prototype derived from wreckage of the Titan Taranis, drawing scepticism from the incumbent but support from thousands of pilots who helped retrieve materials.
