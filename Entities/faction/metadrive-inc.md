## Biography

MetaDrive Inc. was a Chi Orionis-based technology company specialising in hyperdrive research, whose collapse and acquisition became one of the most intriguing corporate mysteries of the early 34th century. In 3302, independent auditor BigSix discovered financial anomalies including payments to undisclosed suppliers without corresponding checks and balances, placing the company in receivership.

Sirius Corporation unexpectedly intervened to prop up the struggling firm, revealing it had been in licensing talks over MetaDrive's "exciting new technology." The situation grew more bizarre when chairman Femi Dakarai died under unclear circumstances, former employee Raan Corsen stole Dakarai's Imperial Eagle and disappeared, and a series of "exodus documents" relating to Dakarai's affairs could not be located. The audit concluded with Sirius Corporation acquiring MetaDrive's remaining assets, though questions about hidden technology and the circumstances of Dakarai's death persisted. MetaDrive's case remains a textbook example of how corporate espionage, financial irregularities, and mysterious deaths can intertwine in the hypercompetitive technology sector.
