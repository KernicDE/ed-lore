## Biography

The Alliance of Independent Systems was founded in 3230 on Alioth after a successful revolt against Federal corporate exploitation and Imperial occupation. Mic Turner and Meredith Argent proposed a new political unit for systems wishing to exist outside both superpowers. Over the next two decades, more than twenty systems joined. The Alliance is governed by the Alliance Assembly, with real military power resting in the Council of Admirals. Prime Minister Edmund Mahon, elected in 3301, is the only leader to win a second term.

The Alliance relies heavily on corporate partnerships to achieve its goals. Its most significant and controversial agreement is the strategic defence pact with Sirius Corporation, signed in January 3308, which grants the Alliance advanced technology and military support in exchange for economic access. Critics argue this makes the Alliance dangerously dependent on corporate interests. During the Thargoid crisis, the Alliance pursued a diplomatic middle path between Federal militarism and Imperial authoritarianism, helping establish Aegis in 3303 while simultaneously running independent research programmes.
