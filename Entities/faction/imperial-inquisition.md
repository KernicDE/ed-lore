## Biography

The Imperial Inquisition is a traditionalist Imperial faction dedicated to upholding Imperial values and combating threats to the Empire's stability. Led by High Inquisitor Mavia Kain, the faction operates as a religious-military organisation with close ties to Emperor's Grace and other conservative Imperial groups.

In August 3301, High Inquisitor Kain met with Admiral Varrwen Mako Brennus of Emperor's Grace at Lindblad Port in Rishair to discuss the assassination of Emperor Duval and joint military operations against the Kumo Crew in the Pegasi sector. The meeting underscored the faction's role as a coordinator of traditionalist resistance to both external threats and internal dissent. The Imperial Inquisition also campaigned to develop mining infrastructure on Brestla A1, demonstrating its interest in economic expansion alongside its military and ideological activities. The faction's activities illustrate how Imperial conservatism manifests in both diplomatic coordination and direct action against perceived enemies of the state.
