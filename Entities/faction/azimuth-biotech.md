## Biography

Azimuth Biotech is the modern incarnation of Azimuth Biochemicals, a corporation officially dissolved two centuries ago but secretly preserved by its founder Caleb Wycherley and his successors. Operating behind fronts such as Taurus Mining Ventures, Azimuth developed Guardian-based anti-xeno superweapons and conducted Project Seraph — a brutal program to install human pilots into captured Thargoid scouts. The company's true nature was exposed in 3308 when Salvation revealed his identity as Wycherley's successor and the superpower navies learned they had been collaborating with a centuries-old weapons firm.

Azimuth's most notorious operation was the Proteus Wave in HIP 22460, a Guardian electromagnetic pulse device intended to eradicate the Thargoid threat. The weapon malfunctioned catastrophically, killing Salvation and unleashing the Thargoid Maelstroms upon the core systems. Under new management, Azimuth Biotech continues to produce anti-xeno weaponry, though its reputation is permanently stained by the HIP 22460 disaster and the moral bankruptcy of its research methods.
