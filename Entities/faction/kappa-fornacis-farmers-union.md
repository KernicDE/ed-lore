# Kappa Fornacis Farmers Union

The Kappa Fornacis Farmers Union is an agricultural faction based in the Kappa Fornacis system, best known for its involvement in the cultivation of Lucan Onionhead. The union represents farmers who grow the psychoactive plant, which is banned in Federation space but thrives in independent systems. Their activities have brought them into conflict with Federal authorities, who view Onionhead production as illegal drug trafficking. The farmers, however, see it as a legitimate cash crop that supports their local economy. The union's stance has made Kappa Fornacis a flashpoint in the ongoing debate over Federal drug policy and frontier economic autonomy.
