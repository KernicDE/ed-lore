## Biography

The Federal Times is a major independent news organisation headquartered in the Sol system, serving as one of the few genuinely critical media voices within the Federation. Unlike the numerous corporate-controlled newsfeeds that dominate Federal media, the Times operates with a degree of editorial independence that has made it both respected and reviled in Federal political circles.

The Times first gained galactic prominence in 3302 when it reported on the Federal Security Service's seizure of Professor Ishmael Palin's Thargoid research data. Palin had conclusively identified the mysterious "Unknown Ships" as Thargoid vessels, but Federal agents intercepted and confiscated his data before it could be widely disseminated. The Times's coverage exposed this government interference in scientific research and established its reputation for investigative journalism that challenges official narratives.

Throughout the Thargoid conflicts, the Federal Times consistently reported on military misconduct, government secrecy, and the human cost of the war. It published leaked documents from the Proactive Detection Bureau revealing the extent of civilian surveillance, interviewed whistleblowers from within the Federal Navy, and provided some of the most detailed coverage of the Starship One disaster and subsequent conspiracy trial. Its reporters have faced harassment, surveillance, and legal threats from Federal agencies, but the publication has maintained its editorial line.

The Times's relationship with the Pilots Federation is particularly notable. As an independent outlet, it frequently covers stories involving independent pilots — from heroism during station evacuations to allegations of piracy and smuggling. Its ALERT coverage provides crucial information to pilots responding to crises, and its investigative work has occasionally intersected with Pilots Federation operations.

In the broader galactic media landscape, the Federal Times represents the Federal counterpart to the Empire's Imperial Herald and the Alliance Tribune. While each outlet carries its own biases, the Times's commitment to holding Federal power accountable — even at significant risk to its staff — has earned it a reputation as one of the most reliable sources for uncensored news in Federal space.
