## Biography

The Federal Times is a major independent news organisation serving as one of the few genuinely critical media voices within the Federation. Unlike corporate-controlled newsfeeds, it operates with editorial independence that has made it both respected and reviled in political circles.

The Times gained prominence in 3302 by exposing the Federal Security Service's seizure of Professor Palin's Thargoid research data — government interference in scientific research that established its reputation for challenging official narratives. Throughout the Thargoid conflicts, it consistently reported on military misconduct, PDB surveillance abuses, and the Starship One conspiracy. Reporters have faced harassment and legal threats from Federal agencies.

In the broader galactic media landscape, the Federal Times represents the Federal counterpart to the Empire's Imperial Herald and the Alliance Tribune. Its commitment to holding Federal power accountable has earned it a reputation as one of the most reliable sources for uncensored news in Federal space.
