## Biography

GalCop—formally the New Galactic Cooperative of Worlds—is a faction founded on principles of interstellar cooperation and non-violence. In May 3302, GalCop representative Jahena Yar Savor issued a statement denouncing the ongoing violence in the Neits system between Federal and Imperial factions, appealing to both sides to end hostilities in the spirit of unity. The organisation's commitment to diplomatic solutions over military confrontation distinguished it from the more aggressive factions vying for power.

GalCop gained wider recognition when it became one of five major factions competing in the Dangerous Games of 3302, a semi-official competition to determine which rising faction would join the galaxy's major powers. Alongside the Alliance Elite Diplomatic Corps, Border Coalition, Interstellar Communist Union, and Social Eleu Progressive Party, GalCop sought to demonstrate that cooperative governance could command the same respect as military might or economic dominance. Although the faction did not ultimately win, its participation established it as a credible political force.
