Silver Legal Group emerged as a key ally of Core Dynamics during the corporate crisis of 3307, when Jupiter Division attempted to seize control of the Federal shipbuilding giant. Operating from the Victory's Forge megaship in the Ts'ao Tach system, this organisation provided crucial legal and paramilitary support to Core Dynamics' loyalist faction. Their involvement transformed what might have been a purely corporate dispute into a system-wide conflict requiring independent pilot intervention.

Working alongside Core Dynamics forces, Silver Legal Group engaged Jupiter Division in sustained combat throughout Ts'ao Tach. Their eventual victory over Jupiter Division proved decisive in preventing the hostile takeover, preserving Core Dynamics' independence and corporate structure. The conflict demonstrated how legal and security firms could wield considerable military influence when aligned with major corporate interests.
