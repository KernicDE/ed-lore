## Biography

Alliance Interpol serves as the primary law enforcement and intelligence agency of the Alliance of Independent Systems. In 3304, Chief Inspector Kay Kilbride represented the organisation on the tri-superpower taskforce investigating the League of Reparation, a terrorist group targeting INRA descendants, until she was lured into a trap and murdered by the League along with four fellow agents.

Deputy Inspector Mara Klatt assumed the liaison role and helped dismantle the League, charging its coordinator Riri McAllister with terrorism-related crimes. Alliance Interpol's expertise in cross-superpower cooperation was again called upon in 3307 when the Affiliated Counter-Terrorism unit was formed to combat the NMLA, with Inspector Klatt serving as one of ACT's three joint leaders alongside the Imperial Internal Security Service and Federal Intelligence Agency.
