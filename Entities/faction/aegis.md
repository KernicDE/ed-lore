## Biography

Aegis was established in August 3303 as humanity's first organised response to the Thargoid threat, uniting scientists and military personnel from all three superpowers. Professor Alba Tesreau directed scientific operations while Admiral Aden Tanner coordinated military affairs across divisions including Aegis Research, Aegis Defence, and the Eagle Eye surveillance network.

In its early years, Aegis achieved significant progress: mapping barnacle sites, developing anti-xeno missiles and shutdown field neutralisers, and establishing forward laboratories in the Pleiades. Its meta-alloy research proved crucial for repairing stations damaged in Thargoid attacks.

The turning point came in 3307, when the megaship Alexandria vanished with 4,000 crew while transporting Guardian artefacts. The Baumann Report recommended complete dissolution, citing complacency and failure to develop new weaponry. Though wreckage logs later revealed Azimuth sabotage, the superpowers rejected appeals for reinstatement. Aegis was formally dismantled in January 3308, its functions devolved to national militaries. Its collapse created the vacuum that Salvation exploited.
