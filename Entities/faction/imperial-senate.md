## Description

The Imperial Senate consists of 1,000 Senators who answer only to the Emperor. Sessions are held in Senate House on Capitol, though most Senators attend virtually. The Senate is routinely presided over by the Chancellor — currently Anders Blaine — while the Leader of the House (Zemina Torval) assumes control when both Emperor and Chancellor are absent.

Senators represent a hierarchical chain of patronage spanning systems and interest groups. While structurally flat by law, the Senate operates through power blocks led by influential Senators whose voting power is determined by the number of citizens they represent. The Senate holds three core powers: legislative authority to change Imperial law, financial access to the Imperial treasury, and electoral responsibility to choose a new Emperor when no clear heir exists.

The Senate's history in the 34th century has been turbulent. Following Emperor Hengist Duval's assassination in 3301, it elected Arissa Lavigny-Duval as the 16th Emperor. During the NMLA crisis of 3306-3307, the Senate divided over whether to negotiate with Marlinist colonies or declare martial law. In 3308, the Lords of Restoration scandal revealed that a secret cabal of Senators had abused senatorial protection to abduct Emperor Arissa herself — one of the most profound breaches of trust in the Senate's history.
