## Biography

The Imperial Guard is an elite legion of warriors under the direct personal command of the Emperor, traditionally seen performing ceremonial duties on Capitol but capable of serving as a highly trained fighting force. Their primary mandate is the protection of the Emperor and the Imperial Family, a responsibility that has grown increasingly critical amid rising internal and external threats.

The Guard has been central to several pivotal moments in recent Imperial history. In 3301 they responded to the assassination attempt on Emperor Hengist Duval during his wedding ceremony. In 3305 they executed Nova Imperium sympathisers on the Senate floor, and in 3308 Chancellor Blaine ordered them into battle against Darkwater Inc. to rescue Emperor Arissa from captivity. Imperial Guard pilots successfully defended the Emperor's convoy during a 3310 ambush in the Cubeo system, reaffirming their reputation as the Empire's ultimate protectors.
