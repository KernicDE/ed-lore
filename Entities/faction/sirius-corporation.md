# Sirius Corporation

## Role
One of the galaxy's oldest and most powerful mega-corporations. Specialises in exploration, logistics, and colonisation. Operates largely independently of the three superpowers.

## Character
Sirius is methodical, patient, and forward-thinking. Unlike other corporations that seek immediate profit, Sirius invests in long-term infrastructure — colonial outposts, exploration data networks, and terraformable worlds. Their partnership with Universal Cartographics demonstrates their strategic approach to expansion.

## Timeline of Significance
- **3301-02-23:** Announces plans for new colonial outposts at the edges of occupied space; offers rewards for exploration data
- **3301-02-28:** Reports 8 new colonisation candidate systems identified
- **3301-03-02:** Completes reconnaissance phase of colonisation project

## Key Operations
- Colonial outpost establishment
- Exploration data acquisition and analysis
- Partnership with Universal Cartographics

## Modern Status (3312)
Sirius Corporation continues to expand its influence, eventually becoming one of the "Big Three" mega-corporations alongside Core Dynamics and Gutamaya Shipyards. Their early 3301 colonisation projects lay groundwork for later expansions into the Pleiades and beyond. CEO Li Yong-Rui eventually becomes a Power in his own right.
