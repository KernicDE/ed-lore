## Biography

Sirius Corporation was founded in 2350, growing rich supplying Federation and Empire war fleets. Its development of hyperdrive technology established it as a foundational pillar of human expansion. The corporation maintains a dominant position in power generation and frame shift drive manufacturing, negotiating "under the Sirius Rule" and applying "the Sirius Convention" in large-scale contracts.

Under CEO Dr. Li Yong-Rui — a prodigy with doctorates in physics, politics, and economics — Sirius has transcended corporate neutrality to play an active diplomatic role. In 3306, Li Yong-Rui proposed constructing the Marlinist Colonies to defuse the refugee crisis; in 3307, he successfully bid to host the Galactic Summit at Patterson Enterprise. The following year, Sirius negotiated a controversial defence pact with the Alliance, providing advanced technology and military support.

Sirius Corporation's fleet includes megaships and a private navy. Its influence has led analysts to describe it as "the fourth superpower" — a label that increasingly reflects reality under Li Yong-Rui's strategic expansion.
