## Biography

Sirius Corporation was founded in 2350, growing rich supplying the war fleets of the Federation and Empire during their centuries of conflict. Its development of commercially viable hyperdrive technology established it as a foundational pillar of human expansion, and to this day it maintains a dominant position in power generation and frame shift drive manufacturing. The corporation's internal culture emphasises fair dealing: it negotiates "under the Sirius Rule," never referring to rival offers, and applies "the Sirius Convention" — a hybrid of local and Sirius law using whichever provision is strictest — in large-scale infrastructure contracts.

Dr. Li Yong-Rui, born in 3198 to the Li dynasty that has been connected to Sirius Corporation since its founding, became CEO sometime before mid-3301. A prodigy who held doctorates in physics, politics, and economics by age 25, he rose to Vice President of Forecasting by 35 before being appointed to lead Sirius Gov. Under his leadership, Sirius Corporation has transcended its traditional corporate neutrality to play an active diplomatic and political role.

In late 3306, as the Marlinist refugee crisis threatened to spark war between the Federation and Empire, Li Yong-Rui personally intervened with a proposal: Sirius would construct independent colonies for the refugees in uninhabited systems. Both superpowers accepted, and the Marlinist Colonies were completed in January 3307. When Simguru Pranav Antal of Utopia proposed a Galactic Summit, Li Yong-Rui counter-proposed that Sirius host it — and won. The summit opened in February 3307 at Patterson Enterprise in the Sirius system, producing the Cornelius-Lasky Convention on counter-terrorism cooperation, though the more significant Sirius Treaty on anti-Thargoid cooperation was shelved after the Nine Martyrs NMLA attack.

In 3304, Sirius Corporation attempted to acquire Guardian-human technology by offering engineer Ram Tah a partnership, which he rejected. When mercenaries subsequently attacked Ram Tah's Phoenix Base, an internal inquiry revealed that junior Sirius officers had colluded with them unofficially. Li Yong-Rui issued a public apology, confirmed the officers would face life imprisonment, and withdrew the offer. In 3308, Sirius Corporation negotiated a defence pact with the Alliance, providing technological and military support that significantly boosted the Alliance's defensive capabilities.

Sirius Corporation's fleet includes megaships and a private navy, and its engineering division operates shipyards across its territory. The corporation's influence has led some analysts to describe it as "the fourth superpower" — a label that, under Li Yong-Rui's strategic expansion, increasingly reflects reality.
