## Biography

The Empire began as an idealistic project in 2292, when Marlin Duval founded the Republic of Achenar on Capitol as an egalitarian alternative to Federal corporate control. Her vision died with her in 2296; her brother Henson seized power, disbanded the ruling council, and declared himself first Emperor in 2320. The Duval dynasty has ruled ever since, with the current Emperor Arissa Lavigny-Duval elected by the Senate in 3301.

The Empire's independence was forged in the Battle of Achenar (2324-2325), when a Federal invasion fleet under Admiral Richard Morgan was annihilated by Henson Duval's forces. The victory secured the Empire's autonomy and triggered a crisis in Federal leadership that contributed to the capital's relocation to Mars. The Empire is governed by a hereditary Emperor and the Imperial Senate of 1,000 Senators, with a strictly stratified "cliens" society based on ancient Roman patronage. Imperial slavery is legal and culturally entrenched, while status and honour are the currency of power.

The Empire has faced existential external and internal threats in the 34th century. It joined the Federation and Alliance in establishing Aegis in 3303 to combat the Thargoids, though superpower rivalry continued. The NMLA terrorist campaign of 3306-3308 exposed deep fractures, culminating in the revelation that the Lords of Restoration — a secret cabal of Senators — had orchestrated Emperor Arissa's abduction in 3307.
