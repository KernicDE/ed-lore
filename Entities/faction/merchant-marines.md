# Merchant Marines

The Merchant Marines are a faction focused on trade, logistics, and commercial shipping operations across multiple systems. The organisation provides transport services for commodities, supplies, and personnel, operating in both core and frontier regions. Like many merchant factions, they maintain armed vessels for protection against piracy and have occasionally been drawn into larger conflicts when trade routes come under threat. Their activities reflect the vital role that independent logistics providers play in keeping the galactic economy functioning.
