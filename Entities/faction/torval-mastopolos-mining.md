## Biography

Torval-Mastopolos Mining is a major Imperial mining corporation formed through the merger of Senator Zemina Torval's personal mining interests and the Mastopolos Mining corporation. The company controls extensive mineral extraction operations across Imperial space and beyond, benefiting from both Torval's senatorial influence and Mastopolos's established industrial infrastructure. It is one of the largest employers in the Empire's extractive sector.

The corporation became a flashpoint in 3307 when Senator Torval accused Mastopolos Mining of financial irregularities and attempted to dissolve the partnership. The dispute escalated into a public corporate conflict, with both sides mobilising political and economic resources. The affair exposed the fragility of alliances between Imperial nobility and corporate interests, even as it demonstrated the substantial power that individual Senators wield over galactic commerce. Torval-Mastopolos Mining remains active, though the internal tensions between its founding partners have never been fully resolved.
