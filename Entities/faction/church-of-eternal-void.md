## Biography

The Church of Eternal Void is a religious organisation that worships the Guardians and emerged as a vocal opponent of the Far God cult during the Thargoid conflicts of 3304. Cardinal Hieronymous declared war on the Far God cult, vowing to "expunge their poisonous creed and purge them from the galaxy." The Church saw a dramatic upswing in support following this declaration, capitalising on public animosity toward Thargoid worshippers.

Dr Alfred Ulyanov, a leading exotheologian, noted that the Church offered hope through its binary theology of Guardians versus Thargoids, positioning itself as a force for good against alien evil. The Church's campaign against the Far God cult raised questions about the limits of religious freedom during wartime, with security forces reminding citizens that the Far God sect was legally recognised and hostility against its members was criminal.
