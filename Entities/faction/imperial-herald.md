## Biography

The Imperial Herald is the Empire's most influential news organisation, functioning as an organ of state whose editorial line generally aligns with Imperial policy while serving as a forum for competing political factions.

Its coverage of the 3301 succession crisis established its reputation for detailed political reporting, including the definitive profile of Brendan Paul Darius — tracing his upbringing as a client of Senator Patreus, his military service, and his rise to patron status before assassinating Emperor Hengist Duval.

The Herald maintains bureaus across Imperial space, covering Senate sessions, military campaigns, and Imperial Family activities. While fundamentally supportive of the establishment, it has published critical investigations regarding the NMLA threat and Marlinist dissidents. Its coverage of Princess Aisling Duval is extensive, reflecting both her popularity and establishment concern about her reformist agenda.

In the broader galactic media landscape, the Herald represents the Empire's perspective, emphasising the stability of Imperial governance in contrast to Federal democracy and Alliance consensus politics.
