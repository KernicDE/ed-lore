## Biography

EG Pilots is an independent pilot faction that won the Dangerous Games Wildcard competition in June 3302, earning the right to compete against five major factions for official power status. The group accumulated the most conductive fabrics across three heats of the competition, demonstrating their ability to attract considerable support from the independent pilot community.

Following their wildcard victory, EG Pilots entered the main Dangerous Games to compete with the Alliance Elite Diplomatic Corps, Border Coalition, GalCop, Interstellar Communist Union and Social Eleu Progressive Party. Reporter Trinity Avon of The Sovereign noted that while EG Pilots had proven their organisational capacity, the true test would be whether they could challenge established powers for a permanent place among the galaxy's most influential factions.
