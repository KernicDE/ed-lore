The Hamsters constitute an independent group of explorers and investigators based in the Tionisla system, a region renowned for its historic orbital graveyard of decommissioned vessels. In late 3302, members of this organisation discovered a cryptic rhyme concealed within exploration data gathered by the Children of Raxxla, triggering widespread community interest and collaborative investigation. Their finding demonstrated how independent groups could contribute meaningfully to the galaxy's ongoing mysteries through careful analysis of seemingly routine data.

The group's activities spanned several months as they worked alongside other independent investigators to decipher the meaning behind the concealed message. Their base in Tionisla provided convenient access to both the famous graveyard and well-travelled routes connecting to core systems. Through their discovery, The Hamsters illustrated the enduring appeal of collaborative exploration and the potential for hidden knowledge to emerge from unexpected sources within the vast dataset of human exploration.
