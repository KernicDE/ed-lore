# Ross 310 Cartel

The Ross 310 Cartel is a criminal organisation operating within the Ross 310 system. In March 3304, the Cartel launched an open bid to overthrow the system's controlling faction, Ross 310 Natural Services, and seize control. Described by opponents as well equipped and highly organised, the Cartel appealed to independent combat pilots for military support in its campaign. The conflict was eventually resolved when system security forces, reinforced by allied pilots, repelled the Cartel's takeover attempt and restored order to Ross 310.
