# Los Chupacabras

Los Chupacabras is a faction operating in frontier systems, named after a mythical creature from old Earth folklore. The group has engaged in various activities ranging from resource extraction to local system defence, reflecting the adaptable nature of frontier factions that must diversify to survive. While not one of the galaxy's major powers, Los Chupacabras represents the type of independent organisation that fills governance gaps in less populated regions, often becoming the de facto authority in systems overlooked by superpowers.
