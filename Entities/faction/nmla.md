## Biography

The Neo-Marlinist Liberation Army (NMLA) emerged in 3306 as the armed wing of a political movement that traces its origins to Marlin Duval, the founder of the Achenar colony who envisioned a democratic republic rather than the Imperial dictatorship that her brother Henson established after her death. For centuries, Marlinism existed as a fringe political philosophy within the Empire, advocating for republican government, individual rights, and an end to the Duval dynasty. The NMLA transformed this ideology into terrorism.

The organisation's operational structure is characterised by extreme compartmentalisation. Individual cells have minimal knowledge of each other, and communications flow through a "dark-comms" network that defied all conventional surveillance efforts until late 3307. The NMLA's bomb-making division, known as Theta Group, was composed of engineers who had been imprisoned at Serene Harbour — an Imperial Intelligence penal facility in the R CrA Sector AF-A d42 system. After escaping, their leader adopted his prisoner designation, "Theta Seven," as both a nom de guerre and a political statement against Imperial dehumanisation of Neo-Marlinists.

The NMLA's signature weapon is the caustic enzyme bomb — customised explosives that incorporate Thargoid corrosives and enzymes, inflicting catastrophic damage on starport infrastructure and causing massive casualties. These bombs were first deployed in September 3306 against four Imperial starports, killing over sixty thousand people. The organisation's deadliest attack, codenamed "Nine Martyrs" by Theta Seven, occurred on 11 March 3307: simultaneous bombings of nine starports across all three superpowers, including Dawes Hub in Achenar, Donaldson in Alioth, and Li Qing Jao in Sol. Over half a million people died, with hundreds of thousands more injured. The attack was staged through the Mudhrid system, where NMLA infiltrators loaded bomb components onto cargo transports bound for the target starports.

The Nine Martyrs attack had profound political consequences. It derailed the Galactic Summit in the Sirius system, where the superpowers had been negotiating the Sirius Treaty on anti-Thargoid cooperation. In its aftermath, the Cornelius-Lasky Convention established the Affiliated Counter-Terrorism unit (ACT) — a joint task force drawing on the superpowers' security services to hunt down the NMLA. ACT's investigation was slow and costly; Captain Niamh Seutonia, one of its original co-leaders, was killed in an NMLA ambush in March 3307.

By November 3307, ACT had captured most of Theta Group, but Theta Seven remained at large until December 3307, when he was cornered aboard the Far God cult megaship Sacrosanct in the Mudhrid system. Rather than surrender, he destroyed the vessel, killing himself and thousands of hostages. ACT subsequently focused on dismantling remaining cells and investigating the NMLA's broader support network, which included Neo-Marlinist sympathisers in the Marlinist Colonies and potentially within the Imperial Senate itself.

The NMLA's ideology — that the Duval dynasty has crushed republican ideals for a thousand years — resonated with enough Imperial citizens to sustain a multi-year terror campaign. Its violence, however, alienated the mainstream Marlinist movement, which repeatedly disavowed the NMLA and cooperated with counter-terrorism efforts. The tension between peaceful Marlinist reformers and the NMLA's revolutionary violence remains a defining fault line in Imperial politics.
