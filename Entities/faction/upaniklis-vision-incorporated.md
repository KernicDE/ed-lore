## Biography

Upaniklis Vision Incorporated is the controlling faction of the Upaniklis system. In 3305, it coordinated the local response to one of the most remarkable discoveries in recent history: the Golconda, an inhabited generation ship that had drifted into the system after more than a millennium of isolation.

Itsuko Harada of Upaniklis Vision Incorporated helped organise medical and material aid for the Golconda's crew, who were suffering from a virus called "the wasting kiss." The faction also offered bounty vouchers to deter pirate activity during the relief effort. As the story attracted superpower attention, Upaniklis Vision Incorporated remained one of the few organisations permitted to interact directly with the Golconda's representatives.
