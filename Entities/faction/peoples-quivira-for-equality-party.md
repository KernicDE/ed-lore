## Biography

The People's Quivira for Equality Party found itself at the centre of one of the most brutal debt-enforcement wars in Imperial history when Senator Denton Patreus initiated legal action against Quivira in 3301. The system had taken bridge loans from Silver Universal Plc during a recession, and when rising rates left the party unable to pay, Quivira Electronics Plc — acting on Patreus's behalf — seized Stapledon Dock and began hiring freelance reclamation agents to extract the debt by force.

Rather than surrender, the party established a defence fund with the Bank of Zaonce and rallied hundreds of independent and Federal pilots to its cause. Though Imperial forces eventually secured Godel Dock and ended the war, the party succeeded in evacuating millions of civilians to Summerland and Persephone. In a remarkable political pivot, the defeated party pledged itself to Princess Aisling Duval's service — transforming a story of military defeat into one of humanitarian survival and strategic realignment.
