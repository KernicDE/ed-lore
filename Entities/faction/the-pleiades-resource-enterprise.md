The Pleiades Resource Enterprise operated as a recovery and logistics organisation active in the Pleiades sector during the intensifying Thargoid conflict of 3304. Working in coordination with the Merope Expeditionary Fleet, the Enterprise specialised in rescue operations and resource extraction from systems experiencing heavy Thargoid activity. Their operations targeted regions such as Pleiades Sector OI-T c3-7 and Arietis Sector XE-Z b4, where concentrations of alien vessels posed severe threats to civilian and military personnel alike.

While the Merope Expeditionary Fleet managed combat engagements, the Pleiades Resource Enterprise coordinated evacuation and recovery efforts, ensuring that survivors and valuable materials were extracted from besieged outposts. This division of responsibilities reflected the multifaceted nature of the Thargoid crisis, which demanded both military resistance and humanitarian response. The Enterprise's work highlighted the critical role that civilian logistics organisations played in supporting front-line operations during humanity's most significant external threat.
