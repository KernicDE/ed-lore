## Biography

The Imperial Internal Security Service (IISS) is the Empire's primary intelligence and law enforcement agency, responsible for domestic security, counter-intelligence and monitoring threats to Imperial stability. It operates with broad authority throughout Imperial space and answers directly to the Imperial Senate, though its relationship with the Emperor varies depending on the reign. The IISS maintains extensive surveillance networks and intelligence assets across Imperial systems.

In 3306, the IISS played a central role in the hunt for the Neo-Marlinist Liberation Army (NMLA), coordinating with the Imperial Navy to track the terrorist organisation's cells and supply networks. Its operatives conducted raids, interrogations and surveillance operations that gradually dismantled NMLA infrastructure. However, the service also faced criticism for failing to prevent the bombing of several Imperial stations and for the continuing evasion of the NMLA's leadership. The IISS's mixed record in counter-terrorism operations exposed the limits of even Imperial intelligence capabilities against a decentralised and ideologically motivated threat.
