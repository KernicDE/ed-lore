# Newton Dock Medical

Newton Dock Medical is the medical authority responsible for healthcare at Newton Dock in the BD-02 4304 system. Led by Chief Medical Officer Carl Simmons, the organisation found itself at the epicentre of the Cerberus Plague outbreak in July 3301. Simmons coordinated initial containment efforts and confirmed the disease's spread to neighbouring systems including Dajoar, Eleu, and 20 Ophiuchi. Newton Dock Medical ultimately played a pivotal role in ending the crisis when Simmons verified that Ceremonial Heike Tea—offered originally just to lift patients' spirits—contained ingredients capable of curing the pathogen.
