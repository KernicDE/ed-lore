## Biography

The Intergalactic Naval Reserve Arm (INRA) was a covert tri-superpower taskforce established in 3125 during the First Thargoid War, charged with developing strategies to defeat the alien threat by any means necessary. Operating in extreme secrecy, INRA pursued unethical research including biological weapons, human experimentation, and the deliberate sacrifice of colonies to test Thargoid responses. Its methods were so notorious that the organisation was officially disbanded and its records sealed — though many of its personnel and technologies simply disappeared into private hands.

INRA's shadow stretches across centuries of galactic history. The discovery of abandoned INRA outposts in the 3300s revealed the full horror of its programmes, sparking public outrage and providing ideological fuel for the League of Reparation's terrorist campaign against INRA descendants. More disturbingly, evidence emerged that Azimuth Biochemicals — the corporation behind the anti-xeno figure known as Salvation — was a direct successor to INRA's research division, suggesting that the organisation's unethical legacy survived long after its official dissolution.
