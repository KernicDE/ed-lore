## Biography

The NMLA emerged in 3306 as the armed wing of Marlinism — a political tradition tracing back to Marlin Duval's vision of a democratic republic. The organisation operated through extreme compartmentalisation, using a "dark-comms" network that eluded conventional surveillance. Its bomb-making division, Theta Group, was led by John Tyburn ("Theta Seven"), a former prisoner at Serene Harbour who adopted his designation as a political statement.

The NMLA's signature caustic enzyme bombs first struck four Imperial starports in September 3306, killing over sixty thousand. The deadliest attack, "Nine Martyrs" on 11 March 3307, involved simultaneous bombings of nine starports across all three superpowers including their capitals, killing over half a million people and derailing the Galactic Summit. The Cornelius-Lasky Convention established ACT in response, though co-leader Captain Niamh Seutonia was killed in an ambush.

Theta Seven was cornered aboard the Far God megaship Sacrosanct in December 3307, destroying the vessel rather than surrendering. While the NMLA was ultimately dismantled, its ideology exposed deep tensions within Imperial society between the Duval dynasty and republican dissent.
