# Revolution Incorporated

Revolution Incorporated is a political faction active within the Empire, operating at the intersection of corporate interests and Imperial power structures. The organisation has been involved in various political manoeuvres, leveraging economic influence to advance its agenda within the competitive landscape of Imperial politics. While less visible than major Imperial institutions, Revolution Incorporated represents the type of corporate-political hybrid entity that thrives in the Empire's feudal-capitalist system.
