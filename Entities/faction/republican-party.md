# Republican Party

The Republican Party is one of the Federation's two major political parties, alongside the Liberal Party led by Shadow President Felicia Winters. Republicans advocate for increased military spending, corporate alignment, and strong executive authority. President Zachary Hudson has been the party's dominant figure since 3301, steering its agenda toward militarism and security expansion. The party has faced internal dissent over Hudson's more authoritarian measures, including his attempt to remove the eight-year presidential term limit in 3308. Despite this, analysts believe Hudson maintains the support of his Cabinet and a majority of Republican congressmen.
