## Biography

The Kumo Crew is the largest pirate syndicate in human space, led by Archon Delaine from the Pegasi Sector with its primary base in Harma. By 3301, Delaine had already established the Crew as a major force, impounding tens of thousands of ships to eliminate rivals. The Empire's "Pegasi Pirate War" to dislodge him degenerated into a war of attrition, demonstrating logistical capabilities that rivalled national militaries.

What distinguishes the Kumo Crew is Delaine's political ambition. In February 3307, he gatecrashed the Galactic Summit at Patterson Enterprise, demanding recognition as a delegate and delivering his infamous "crown of bones" speech wearing a macabre headpiece of his enemies' remains. The incident forced superpowers to acknowledge that pirate power could not be ignored.

The syndicate engages in drug trafficking, slavery, arms smuggling, and protection rackets, yet Delaine also maintains internal order in Kumo-controlled systems — providing basic services that lead analysts to describe his domain as a "pirate kingdom." The Empire considers him an existential threat, while the Alliance has occasionally found him useful as a counterweight to Imperial expansion.
