## Biography

The Canonn Interstellar Research Group is an independent scientific organisation founded by Dr Arcanonn that has been at the forefront of every major xenological discovery since 3301. Comprised largely of volunteer Commanders and researchers, Canonn was the first to confirm the existence of alien barnacle structures in the Pleiades Nebula and to decode the distorted transmissions of Unknown Artefacts. Its decentralized, crowdsourced approach to research allowed it to operate faster than official superpower agencies, often publishing findings that forced governments to acknowledge threats they preferred to ignore.

Canonn established key facilities including a research base at Varati and maintained a close presence at Obsidian Orbital in Maia during the early Thargoid contact period. While Aegis eventually supplanted it as the primary xeno-research body, Canonn's early work laid the foundation for all subsequent understanding of Thargoid biology, behaviour, and technology.
