## Biography

The Federal Navy traces its origins to the Federation's founding in 2242. It suffered a historic humiliation at the Battle of Achenar in 2325, where an expeditionary fleet was annihilated by Henson Duval's forces — a defeat that ensured the Empire's independence and prompted the Federal capital's relocation to Mars.

By the 34th century, the Navy's pinnacle vessel is the Farragut Battle Cruiser, a two-kilometre-long capital ship whose deployment is generally sufficient to end all but the largest engagements. Its relationship with civilian government has been fraught: during the Lugh campaign in 3301, President Halsey's order to attack an unarmed refugee convoy killed an estimated 9,322 civilians, causing senior commanders to shift loyalty to Zachary Hudson. Fleet Admiral Lucas Vincent's subsequent conspiracy with Core Dynamics CEO Jupiter Rochester to destroy Starship One — exposed in 3307 — further damaged the Navy's reputation.

During the Thargoid conflicts, the Federal Navy contributed to Aegis and later operated independent anti-xeno task forces after Aegis's dissolution in 3308. It remains the dominant military power in Federal space, though its association with the Hudson administration's controversial policies continues to shadow its standing.
