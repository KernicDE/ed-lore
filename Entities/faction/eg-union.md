## Biography

EG Union is the military and political faction led by Yuri Grom, operating as the power base for his independent expansionist agenda. In October 3307, EG Union forces defeated Delta Pavonis Galactic Interstellar in a system conflict, capturing the Federal megaship Freedom's Foundation as spoils of war.

The victory represented a direct military confrontation between Grom's forces and Federal auxiliaries, with Grom publicly denouncing President Hudson as "the corrupt heart of the Federation's rotting carcass." EG Union serves as both the governing authority in Grom-controlled systems and the military arm of his challenge to Federal influence in the frontier, embodying his strategy of leveraging independent pilot support to secure territorial gains against superpower opposition.
