## Biography

Adle's Armada is a Federal-leaning vigilante group that traces its origins to a classified inter-factional combat force from which Post Commander Tyllerius Adle pulled his detachment in 3200, rebranding it as an independent militia. Headquartered in the Ngaliba system, the group historically defended the defenceless against forces that sought to do evil in the galaxy.

In June 3301, Adle's Armada declared itself a sovereign power independent of all galactic powers, operating primarily in the Ngaliba, Mbukuravi, and Kremainn systems with its own system of governance based on its own rules of law. The group recruits only the finest combat pilots with clean consciences and has intervened in conflicts across Federal and Imperial space. In July 3301, the Armada laid siege to the League of Hel to block an illegal arms deal between Cosmic State's Hammer Arms and Emperor's Grace, with Tyllerius Adle III condemning the trade as a tyranny that took "the food out of people's mouths." The Armada's declaration of sovereignty established a unique independent power born from Federal military roots, rejecting all superpower allegiances while maintaining its vigilante ethos.
