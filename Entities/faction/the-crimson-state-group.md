# The Crimson State Group

## Role
The primary political faction advocating for Lugh's independence from Federal rule. Commands overwhelming popular support within the Lugh system.

## Character
The Crimson State Group represents a homegrown independence movement with deep historical roots. Their claim is based on Lugh's original settlement by a sovereign people of shared lineage and religion. They seek diplomatic recognition from the Alliance as a legitimate independent government.

## Timeline of Significance
- **3301-02-22:** Polls show 87% popular support; Ambassador Éamonn Uí Laoghire takes their case to Alioth

## Key Relationships
- **Éamonn Uí Laoghire** — Leading spokesperson and ambassador
- **Federation** — Opposing power; Federal Navy is the primary obstacle to independence
- **Alliance** — Potential ally; appeal made for diplomatic recognition

## Modern Status (3312)
The Lugh independence crisis becomes a test case for frontier sovereignty. The Crimson State Group's appeal to the Alliance sets a precedent for how independent systems seek protection from Federal domination. Their struggle inspires later independence movements across the frontier.
