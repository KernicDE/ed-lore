# Pleiades Concordat

The Pleiades Concordat is a theocratic faction that controls the Panjabell system and is devoted to the Far God cult. Unlike the pacifist mainstream of the cult, the Concordat has established a formal political structure around their apocalyptic faith. The faction has welcomed migrating chapters of the Far God sect, providing them sanctuary from persecution by anti-xeno activists and Federal intelligence agencies. Erik Gunnarson of the Wallglass Investigations Agency noted that the Concordat's control of Panjabell suggested they might be preparing something "bigger—or stranger—than we've seen before."
