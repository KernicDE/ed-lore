## Biography

Lp 726-6 Free is a Federal faction that declared independence from the Federation in July 3307, citing the Proactive Detection Bureau's civilian surveillance laws as an intolerable violation of civil liberties. The secession was inspired by the earlier success of Azaladshu Free, which had broken away after its military victory over Neche Inc allowed it to retain independent control of four systems.

The faction's defection triggered a chain reaction across Federal space, with the Coalition of Uteran and Gluscap Labour following suit and ten systems representing more than a billion people eventually leaving the Federation. The crisis exposed deep fractures within the Federal Accord, with President Zachary Hudson blaming criminal behaviour while Shadow President Felicia Winters argued that the surveillance laws themselves had caused the rebellion.
