## Biography

The Kumo Council is the ruling body of the Kumo Crew syndicate, formed by pirate warlord Archon Delaine to delegate control over his criminal empire. Each member of the council holds the title of corsair and oversees a region or system within Kumo territory, while Delaine's most trusted advisers — known as arch-corsairs — work with him on strategic goals.

In April 3308, Delaine appointed Callan Salamanca as a new arch-corsair following the death of his father Gideon "Soulless" Salamanca, one of the most infamous warlords in the Pegasi sector. The council's inner circle at that time included Vidar Trask, an expert administrator who had been close to Delaine for many years, and Kay Volantyne, originally part of the Blue Viper Club Dredger clan before negotiating its alliance with the Kumo Crew. The council has also assumed control of systems invaded by Thargoids, coordinating anti-xeno initiatives from the Mictlan megaship in the California Nebula — a move that Harrison Gladstone of the Turner Research Group condemned as an illegal occupation of Allied territory. Delaine's use of the Kumo Council reinforces his self-styled position as "sovereign ruler of the Kumo nation," though the council is frequently called upon to justify or deny their regent's brutal excesses.
