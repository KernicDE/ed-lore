## Biography

LP 855-34 is a minor independent system with a population of barely sixty thousand, a democracy controlled by the Sidewinder Syndicate. Its obscurity ended in May 3311 when a platinum rush triggered by local construction demands attracted the attention of two galactic powers: Yuri Grom's independent forces and Felicia Winters' Federal loyalists.

Grom's representative accused Federal factions of "cornering the market" unfairly, and combat zones erupted throughout the system as pilots clashed over strategic mining sites. The conflict, though brief, illustrated how even the smallest systems could become flashpoints in the post-Thargoid War scramble for resources and influence, with Winters' supporters ultimately prevailing.
