## Biography

LHS 277 was a Federal corporate system controlled by the Minutemen faction until June 3307, when it was one of four systems swept up in Azaladshu Free's declaration of independence from the Federation. The secession, driven by opposition to the Proactive Detection Bureau's surveillance of private communications, transformed LHS 277 from a secure Federal holding into a contested battlefield.

President Hudson's deployment of the megaship Fortress Liberty and his order for Neche Inc to engage the secessionists turned the system into a test case for Federal resolve. When Azaladshu Free defeated Federal forces, LHS 277's independence was secured, inspiring additional factions to break away and deepening the most serious constitutional crisis in Federation history.
