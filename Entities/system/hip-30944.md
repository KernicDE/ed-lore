## Biography

HIP 30944 is a low-population Federal extraction system that was the intended safe harbour for the Aegis megaship Alexandria in 3307. After the megaship triggered a Thargoid attack in HIP 16538, Captain Jacob Morales plotted a route to the prison colony in HIP 30944 to reduce the risk of further casualties.

All independent ships were undocked before the Alexandria made its hyperspace jump on the 3rd of June. The megaship never arrived, and all communication ceased. Search teams found no trace of the vessel. Admiral Aden Tanner announced that Captain Morales and over four thousand crewmembers would be listed as missing presumed dead. The loss also severely impacted Aegis's research programme, as the Alexandria had been carrying precious Guardian artefacts.
