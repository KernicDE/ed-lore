## Description

Lugh is a heavily populated agricultural system that became the flashpoint for one of the most notorious independence wars in recent history. In early 3301, the Crimson State Group led by Éamonn Uí Laoghire spearheaded a popular secessionist movement from the Federation, claiming over 87% local support. When Uí Laoghire sought Alliance recognition on Alioth, President Jasmina Halsey ordered the Federal Navy to suppress the uprising, triggering a brutal two-week war.

The conflict is remembered for the massacre of an unarmed refugee convoy fleeing Lugh 6, in which an estimated 9,322 civilians died under Presidential orders. Despite guerrilla resistance by the Sons of Conn and generous payouts to independent pilots, Federal forces stormed Seega Port and crushed the rebellion. The defeat extinguished Lugh's bid for sovereignty and became a defining scandal of Halsey's presidency.
