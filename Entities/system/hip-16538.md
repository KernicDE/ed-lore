## Biography

HIP 16538 is an independent industrial system with a population of over 34 million. In 3307, it became the site of a catastrophic Thargoid attack when the Aegis megaship Alexandria jumped from Delphi to the system for a tritium refuelling stopover en route to Sol.

The megaship was carrying large quantities of Guardian artefacts as part of an Aegis research programme. Admiral Aden Tanner believed the concentrated Guardian technology triggered the Thargoid assault. After independent pilots helped defend the vessel, the Alexandria plotted an escape jump to the low-population system HIP 30944. It vanished in hyperspace and was never heard from again, making HIP 16538 the last known location of one of the most infamous disappearances in recent history.
