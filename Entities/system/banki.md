## Biography

President Jasmina Halsey ordered an all-out assault on the Nijotec region in February 3301, accusing the Nijotec Syndicate of preying on the weak and declaring that criminals would find no sanctuary in Federal space. Banki and BD+03 2338 were among the systems caught in the offensive, having fallen under syndicate control through a combination of bribery and intimidation.

Admiral Vincent later expressed satisfaction with Federal forces' progress in reclaiming both systems. For Banki's half-million inhabitants, primarily supported by tourism and colony operations under the democratic Benelux Commanders, liberation meant a return to independent governance after weeks of criminal occupation. The campaign demonstrated the Federation's willingness to deploy overwhelming naval force against organised crime operating near its borders.
