# Chun Pindit

Chun Pindit is a system that came under Thargoid invasion in 3308 as part of the broader alien escalation preceding the Titan War. The system required coordinated military intervention from independent pilots and superpower forces to repel the Thargoid incursion. Like many systems caught in the path of Thargoid expansion, Chun Pindit suffered significant infrastructure damage and required substantial humanitarian support in the aftermath. The invasion demonstrated how rapidly Thargoid forces could penetrate human-controlled space.
