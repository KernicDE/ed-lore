## Biography

HIP 17692 is an independent system in the Pleiades region with a population of approximately 900,000, controlled by the Anti Xeno Initiative under a patronage government. Located near the frontier of Thargoid space, the system's strategic position made it a focal point for superpower military expansion during the early Thargoid conflict.

In June 3303, the Federation announced plans to establish an asteroid base among the rings of HIP 17692 A4 to monitor Thargoid activity, with the Pleiades Resource Enterprise overseeing construction. The Empire simultaneously announced a competing initiative to bolster its Navy with a new Wells-class Carrier, coordinated by the Merope Expeditionary Fleet. Both superpowers placed open orders for construction materials, turning HIP 17692 into a flashpoint for the escalating rivalry between the Federation and Empire as each sought to consolidate influence in the Pleiades. The completed Federal outpost provided a permanent military presence in a region that would become one of the most contested sectors of the Thargoid war.
