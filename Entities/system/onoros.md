## Biography

Onoros lies within the Witch Head Nebula, an Imperial dictatorship system controlled by The Imperial Exemplars. In October 3306, it was one of five systems swarmed by Thargoid vessels in a sudden resurgence that threatened the entire sixteen-system Witch Head Enclave, which had been colonised the previous year after the discovery of fresh barnacle sites.

Captain Warren Lamar, head of security for the Alliance Expeditionary Pact, issued an urgent call for independent pilots to defend Onoros and its neighbouring systems, warning that the assault could spread into a full-scale invasion. The renewed Thargoid presence demonstrated that humanity's expansion into nebula territories remained precarious, even after initial military successes.
