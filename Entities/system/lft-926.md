## Biography

LFT 926 is an independent democratic system with a high-tech economy, controlled by the Dark Wheel — a faction shrouded in mystery and rumoured connections to the search for Raxxla. In May 3304, this otherwise obscure system became the unlikely venue for the Jailhouse Rock and Blues Music Festival, organised by the local Los Chupacabras faction.

The festival's organisers placed open orders for beer, tobacco, coffee and narcotics, promising rewards to pilots who delivered the commodities to Meredith City. A spokesperson quoted Plato's dictum that "music is a moral law," aspiring to create "the greatest music festival this side of Sagittarius A*." The event demonstrated that even in an era of Thargoid invasion and superpower conflict, frontier communities still found reasons to celebrate.
