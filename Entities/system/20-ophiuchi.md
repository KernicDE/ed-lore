## Biography

When the Cerberus Plague breached quarantine at Newton Dock in BD-02 4304, travellers who had already departed carried the infection to three neighbouring systems. 20 Ophiuchi was one of them, confirming isolated cases in mid-July 3301 alongside Dajoar and Eleu. All three systems declared states of emergency and appealed to bounty hunters and combat pilots to protect their relief convoys from disruption.

The system's rapid response reflected both the severity of the threat and the inadequacy of interstellar disease containment protocols. Governed by the Social Eleu Progressive Party under a cooperative charter, 20 Ophiuchi's industrial economy ground to a halt as quarantine measures took effect. The outbreak served as an early warning of how quickly pathogens could traverse light-years in a galaxy dependent on constant civilian travel.
