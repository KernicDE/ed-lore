## Biography

In June 3302, the Daramo system erupted into a three-way conflict that pitted the Federation against both the Empire and Pirate King Archon Delaine's Kumo Crew. Admiral Yorke of the Federal Navy issued an emergency call to all Federation-aligned pilots, declaring that Daramo held considerable strategic value and would not be surrendered. Captain Danvers of the Imperial Navy responded with equal fervour, claiming Imperial law would protect the system's citizens from Federal mismanagement.

After three days of intense fighting, the Independents of Daramo confirmed Federal victory. Admiral Yorke's post-battle statement proved almost as memorable as the engagement itself, mocking the Empire's origins as "nothing more than a gaggle of clueless colonists on Achenar 6d" while the Federation controlled over a thousand systems. The remark triggered diplomatic fallout that outlasted the battle, demonstrating how even minor territorial disputes could reignite centuries-old superpower animosity.
