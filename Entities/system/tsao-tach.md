## Biography

Ts'ao Tach is a Federal system governed by corporate interests and controlled by the Silver Legal Group, situated in a region that became strategically vital during the corporatist crisis of 3307. In early June 3307, Core Dynamics and the Silver Legal Group engaged Jupiter Division in open conflict within the system, determined to crush the rogue faction that had been gathering support for ex-CEO Jupiter Rochester's vision of corporate rule.

By 10 June 3307, Jupiter Division had been defeated and was subsumed by Core Enterprises, a Core Dynamics subsidiary, marking the effective end of the corporatist insurrection. The battle in Ts'ao Tach demonstrated that the Federal corporate establishment would not tolerate internal rebellion, and the system's role as the final battlefield against Rochester's movement cemented its place in Federal political history.
