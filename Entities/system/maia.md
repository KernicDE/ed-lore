## Biography

Prior to 3301, Maia was a remote, largely uninhabited system on the fringes of the inhabited bubble. That changed with the Unknown Artefact phenomenon. In July 3301, Canonn research in Maia revealed that UAs emitted distorted signals broadcasting the identity of the nearest celestial body, igniting scientific interest in the Pleiades. By December 3301, the "technological plague" had spread to the newly constructed Obsidian Orbital.

In January 3302, a signal received at Obsidian Orbital led pilots to discover barnacle sites producing meta-alloys. Professor Ishmael Palin established a research base and appealed for deliveries, but the campaign turned violent when protestors blockaded the station. Palin's subsequent kidnapping in February 3302 — and the black-market sale of thousands of tonnes of meta-alloys — deepened the mystery surrounding the system.

Maia's strategic importance escalated further in 3302-3303. The Federation and Empire used it as a logistics hub for Pleiades expansion, while the Ant Hill Mob fought off the Federation-aligned Pleiades Resource Enterprise in a proxy war. In September 3303, the system became the site of the first confirmed Thargoid destruction of a Federal capital ship — a Farragut-class Battlecruiser — marking a dramatic escalation in the conflict.
