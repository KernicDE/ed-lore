## Biography

HIP 22460 was an obscure system until it became the site of the most consequential battle of the Second Thargoid War. Its remoteness attracted Azimuth Biochemicals, which established secret research facilities there decades earlier.

In 3307–3308, pilots discovered abandoned Azimuth facilities in HIP 22460 and nearby HIP 26176 — sites of Project Seraph, a programme of horrific human experimentation attempting to install pilots into captured Thargoid scouts. These discoveries provided crucial early evidence linking Salvation to Azimuth's legacy.

The system's defining moment came in August 3308, when Salvation activated the Proteus Wave aboard the Bright Sentinel megaship. The Guardian-derived superweapon initially disabled Thargoid vessels across the system, but triggered a catastrophic counter-attack. The Thargoids deployed previously unknown Titan-class vessels and unleashed a counter-wave that destroyed the Bright Sentinel and killed Salvation. The battle transformed the war, prompting a radical reassessment of anti-xeno strategy and marking the end of the Salvation era.
