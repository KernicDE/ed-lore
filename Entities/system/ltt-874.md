## Biography

LTT 874 is an independent democratic system with a large population and an economy based on agriculture and extraction, controlled by the United German Commanders faction. In October 3301, the Imperial Internal Security Service identified it as one of five systems containing bases belonging to the insurgent organisation Emperor's Dawn, which had assassinated Emperor Hengist Duval.

Senator Denton Patreus dispatched military fleets to LTT 874 as part of a coordinated offensive against the insurgent bases, operating alongside simultaneous campaigns in Namarii and Tabaldak. The battle for the system became a significant front in the Empire's war against the terrorists, with both Imperial forces and Emperor's Dawn offering rewards to attract independent pilots to their respective causes.
