The Nespeleve system is a heavily populated territory home to nearly twenty million inhabitants, with Wilkes Orbital serving as its primary starport and administrative centre. Located in a region with connections to both the Pleiades and core worlds, the system maintains its own defence forces while participating in broader security arrangements. Its substantial population and strategic positioning make it a significant economic and logistical node within its region.

In early 3302, former soldier Rose Trebek established the Joint Security Taskforce with support from Nespeleve authorities, aiming to combat the ruthless Blue Hand Gang operating in the nearby Orulas system. The campaign requested pilot assistance to intercept convoys carrying stolen escape pods and sweep criminal vessels from affected areas. This initiative demonstrated how systems like Nespeleve could serve as bases for cross-jurisdictional security operations, leveraging their resources and infrastructure to address threats that extended beyond their immediate boundaries.
