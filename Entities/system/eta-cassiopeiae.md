## Biography

Nine billion people live under the control of the Marine Forces Special Operations Command in Eta Cassiopeiae, making it one of the most populous Federation systems outside the Sol sector. Its confederate governance structure and agricultural-colony economy belie a heavily militarised administration that placed the system on Aegis's watchlist during the intensifying Thargoid conflicts of 3308–3310.

The system's importance stemmed from its position near the Federal core and its potential as both a population centre at risk and a military staging ground. While never subjected to the direct Thargoid incursions that devastated systems like HIP 22460, Eta Cassiopeiae represented the kind of high-value target that kept Federal strategists awake at night. Its mention alongside superpower and Aegis interests underscored how even well-defended core systems could not be considered safe as the war expanded.
