LTT 9810 is an independent cooperative system of extraordinary scale, home to over seventeen billion inhabitants with a high-security economy built on agriculture and industrial processing. Governed by the Celestial Light Brigade, the system features Gutierrez Terminal among its installations and maintains connections to both the Patrons of Law and the Nehet Silver Ring. Its massive population places it among the most densely inhabited independent territories in the galaxy, demonstrating that cooperative governance models could sustain populations comparable to superpower core worlds.

In April 3301, the system became a focal point for anti-cartel operations when pilots crushed the Mictlan Cartel and Imperial inquisitors extracted information about a previously unknown underworld consultancy believed to be based near LTT 9810. The system served as a mustering point for these operations, with commanders signing up at Gutierrez Terminal. This episode illustrated how even well-populated, high-security systems could harbour criminal networks operating beneath the surface of legitimate commerce, and how coordinated action between independent pilots and official investigators could penetrate such organisations.
