## Biography

HIP 18390 is an independent feudal system with a refinery and extraction economy, located near the California Nebula. In April 3307, it was chosen by Archon Delaine's Kumo Council as one of five sites for new Orbis starports constructed by Kavanagh Spaceframes Ltd, extending the syndicate's reach into strategically significant regions.

The system's selection reflected Delaine's ambition to project pirate power beyond traditional territories. When the campaign concluded successfully, the new starport in HIP 18390 opened for business alongside stations in the Coalsack Nebula, Colonia and near Sothis, demonstrating how criminal infrastructure could expand across the galaxy with the same logistical sophistication as legitimate corporate ventures.
