## Biography

With nearly nineteen billion inhabitants and a refinery-based economy, LP 339-7 might seem an unlikely setting for a nature reserve. Yet in late 3302, Dr Lewis Chapman's Starship Enterprises Corporation chose the system for precisely that purpose, establishing a wildlife sanctuary on LP 339-7 4 A stocked with cloned Earth animals genetically tailored to their new environment.

The project evolved. By 3305, Starship Enterprises had shifted focus to mining operations and the construction of a specialised installation and asteroid base, using the system's substantial resources to expand its industrial footprint. LP 339-7 thus exemplified how corporate ambition could repurpose a system's identity from ecological showcase to manufacturing hub within a few short years.
