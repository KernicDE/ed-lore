## Biography

Mars holds the distinction of being the first planet successfully terraformed by humanity, with the process completed in 2291. An earlier experimental attempt in the 2170s had been abandoned, but the second effort established the template for subsequent terraforming projects across human space.

The planet's political significance increased dramatically after the Battle of Achenar in 2325. To stabilise the Federation amid discontent with Earth's leadership, the capital was relocated from Earth to Mars — a move that maintained Sol's central position while symbolising a fresh start. The Federal Congress now meets on Mars, alongside major government institutions and corporate headquarters.

Mars has been the backdrop for pivotal political moments, most notably in 3301 when Shadow President Zachary Hudson announced his bid for a Vote of No Confidence against President Jasmina Halsey — a successful manoeuvre that installed him as President and shifted Federal politics toward Republican militarism. Despite the shift of galactic commerce to systems like Alioth and Cubeo, Mars remains the administrative centre of the largest superpower in human space.
