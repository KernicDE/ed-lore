# Yum Kamcabi

Yum Kamcabi is a star system that serves as a waypoint for commercial traffic in its region. The system's strategic location has made it relevant to trade routes connecting core and frontier areas. Over the years, Yum Kamcabi has experienced the typical fluctuations of a transit system—periods of prosperity when major trade routes pass through, and quieter times when commercial patterns shift. Its population has adapted to this rhythm, developing service industries that cater to passing traders and travellers.
