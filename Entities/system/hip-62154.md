## Biography

HIP 62154 is an Alliance corporate system with an industrial economy, controlled by the Alliance Expeditionary Pact faction. In April 3307, Archon Delaine's Kumo Council selected it as one of five sites for new Orbis starports, a choice that placed criminal infrastructure deep within Alliance territory in the Coalsack Nebula.

The system's selection was notable for its strategic significance — the Coalsack Nebula was rapidly becoming a focal point of human expansion and Thargoid activity alike. When Kavanagh Spaceframes Ltd completed construction in May 3307, the new starport gave the Kumo Crew a permanent foothold in a region traditionally outside their sphere of influence, demonstrating Delaine's ambition to extend pirate power across the galaxy.
