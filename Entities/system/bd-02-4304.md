## Biography

More than eight billion people called BD-02 4304 home when the Cerberus Plague erupted at Newton Dock in June 3301. The pathogen spread with terrifying speed through the agricultural cooperative, overwhelming medical facilities and forcing system-wide quarantine protocols. Within weeks, the infection had become the most serious public health crisis in recent Federal history.

The plague did not stay contained. Travellers who departed before restrictions took effect carried the disease to Dajoar, Eleu and 20 Ophiuchi, triggering secondary outbreaks across dozens of light-years. Chief Medical Officer Carl Simmons later confirmed these spillover cases, demonstrating how interstellar commerce could transform a localised medical emergency into a regional pandemic before authorities could respond.
