## Biography

Wolf 1301 is an independent system that was affected by the technological plague of 3301–3302. In December 3301, Saunders's Dive in the system reported extensive technical problems including system failures and blackouts, part of a spreading wave of malfunctions affecting multiple starports across inhabited space.

The station's technical team described the same pattern of problems seen at other affected stations, warning that "half the starports in the galaxy are going to be out of order before long." Wolf 1301's experience was eventually traced to Unknown Artefacts, and the station was later restored through the application of meta-alloys. The system exemplified how the technological plague could disrupt high-tech colonies far from the Pleiades Nebula, demonstrating the galaxy-wide reach of Thargoid artefacts.
