# Formidine Rift Mystery

## Summary
The Formidine Rift is a vast region between the galaxy's farthest arms, beyond the Heart and Soul Nebula. For decades it was considered too remote for meaningful exploration until a collective of independent explorers—the Formidine Rifters—began systematically mapping it. Their efforts uncovered one of the most disturbing chapters in human history: the Dynasty Project, a covert superpower mission in the 3270s that sent ships to install beacons in the Rift, the Conflux, and Hawkin's Gap. None of those ships returned. The discovery of their wrecks, logs, and the infamous megaship Zurara revealed a story of abandonment, sacrifice, and government secrecy.

## Phases

### Phase 1: The Expedition (3302-06-18)
Commander Finn McMillan of the Earth Expeditionary Fleet invited explorers to join the Formidine Rifters in mapping the region between the galactic arms. The Rifters were an unlikely collective from various backgrounds and factional allegiances who had put aside political differences for a common goal. McMillan stated they had reason to believe something beyond the galactic arm might change humanity's view of the cosmos, but they needed more explorers to unravel the mystery.

### Phase 2: The Discovery (3303-04-27)
The Rifters' mapping efforts yielded unexpected results. In the remote reaches of the Formidine Rift, explorers discovered wreckage, abandoned settlements, and mysterious beacons dating from the 3270s. The technology was primitive by modern standards—it would have taken a year to complete the journey 30 years prior—but the scale of the operation was immense. Logs indicated a fleet had been dispatched to install a series of beacons across three sectors. None had returned to their point of origin.

### Phase 3: The Dynasty Project (3303-02-03)
The Children of Raxxla published an analysis confirming the mission was called the "Dynasty Project." Its exact purpose remained unclear, but the evidence was undeniable: shipwrecks, beacons, and abandoned settlements across the Formidine Rift, Conflux, and Hawkin's Gap. The Zurara megaship was discovered with its crew dead—apparently abandoned as part of the project. The discovery validated Salomé's claims that these sectors were significant, suggesting she had been silenced for knowing too much about this chapter of hidden history.

## Modern Relevance
The Dynasty Project remains one of the most troubling examples of superpower secrecy. The fact that a fleet was dispatched on a covert mission whose purpose was never disclosed—and that the crews were apparently abandoned to die—raises profound questions about what governments are willing to do in the name of security. The beacons installed during the project continue to transmit, their purpose still unknown. Whether they were navigation aids, warning markers, or something else entirely remains one of the unanswered questions of the Formidine Rift.
