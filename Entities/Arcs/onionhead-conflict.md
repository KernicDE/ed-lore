# Onionhead Conflict

## Summary
The struggle over the cultivation, distribution, and legality of Lucan Onionhead — a psychoactive plant native to Kappa Fornacis. The conflict pits Federal authorities, who ban the drug, against frontier farmers and criminal organisations who see it as a lucrative cash crop.

## Phases

### Phase 1: Tanmark Cultivation (3301-02-24)
The Defence Force of Tanmark and the Farmers Union of Kappa Fornacis successfully increase Onionhead production on Cassie-L-Peia. President Halsey's office condemns the activity and threatens prosecution.

### Phase 2: Tanmark Posse Crackdown (3301-03-03)
Fighting breaks out between the Defence Force of Tanmark and the Tanmark Posse, a criminal gang that had been cultivating modified Onionhead on Luca. The modification prevents natural reproduction, ensuring dependency on the gang's supply. The Defence Force raids Posse holdings.

## Modern Relevance
The Onionhead conflict is an early example of the tension between Federal drug policy and frontier economic reality. It foreshadows later, larger conflicts in Kappa Fornacis and highlights how criminal organisations exploit agricultural communities. President Halsey's hardline stance establishes a Federal position that remains consistent for years.
