# Jaques' Wandering

## Summary
The mysterious journey of Jaques, the cyborg barman, and his mobile starport Jacques Station. After a long absence from populated space, Jaques reappears and begins wandering through systems without clear destination, attracting visitors from all superpowers.

## Phases

### Phase 1: Return (3301-02-12)
Jacques Station is sighted in HIP 110620 after an extended absence. Jaques offers cryptic statements about his travels.

### Phase 2: Aimless Wandering (3301-02-19)
The station moves through HIP 110079 with no announced destination. It becomes a popular social hub for young pilots from all factions, drawn by its mystery and neutral status.

## Modern Relevance
Jaques's wandering is more than a curiosity — it represents the independent spirit of the CMDR community. His station serves as neutral ground in a politically divided galaxy. Eventually, his wanderings lead him to the Colonia region, where Jacques Station becomes the seed of an entirely new human civilisation thousands of light-years from the Bubble. His story embodies the frontier ethos that defines the 34th century.
