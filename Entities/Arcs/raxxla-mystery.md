# Raxxla Mystery

## Summary
Raxxla is the most enduring mystery in human space—a mythical world, station, or location said to hold alien technology, a portal to another place, or secrets that could transform human civilization. First mentioned in 2296, the legend has persisted for over a thousand years, attracting seekers, conspiracy theorists, and secret societies. The Children of Raxxla, based in HR 6421, are the most organised modern searchers. Their research connected Raxxla to the Dynasty Project and Salomé's claims, suggesting the myth may rest on a foundation of suppressed truth.

## Phases

### Phase 1: The Legend (2296-01-01)
The first known reference to Raxxla dates to 2296, making it one of humanity's oldest spacefaring mysteries. Over the centuries, the legend accumulated details: it was a world, or perhaps a station, that held alien technology or a gateway to somewhere else. Various groups claimed to have found it, none convincingly. The Dark Wheel—an organisation with alleged connections to Salomé—was said to have searched for Raxxla for generations. By the 33rd century, most considered it a myth, albeit one that refused to die.

### Phase 2: Modern Search (3303-02-03)
The Children of Raxxla published an analysis of exploration data that revealed the Dynasty Project—a covert mission in the 3270s to install beacons in the Formidine Rift, Conflux, and Hawkin's Gap. Their spokesperson noted that Salomé had indicated these three sectors were significant before her arrest. The discovery of shipwrecks, beacons, and abandoned settlements from the 3270s suggested a massive undertaking whose purpose remained hidden. The Children concluded that Salomé had been killed because she knew why these sectors mattered.

### Phase 3: The Connection (3303-04-27)
Further exploration linked the Dynasty Project to broader questions about superpower secrets. The Zurara megaship was found with its crew dead, apparently abandoned as part of the project. The Children of Raxxla maintained that understanding the Dynasty Project was essential to finding Raxxla, suggesting the myth and the covert mission were different facets of the same hidden history. Whether Raxxla is a real place, a code name for a classified project, or something else entirely remains unknown.

## Modern Relevance
Raxxla represents the human capacity for mystery and the suspicion that powerful forces conceal transformative knowledge. Whether literal or metaphorical, the search for Raxxla has driven exploration into regions that might otherwise have been ignored. The Children of Raxxla's research methods—crowdsourced data analysis, open-source exploration—also demonstrate how independent organisations can challenge official narratives. Even if Raxxla never exists, the search itself has expanded human knowledge of the galaxy's remote reaches.
