# Sirius Expansion

## Summary
Sirius Corporation's methodical colonisation project, launched in early 3301. The project involves crowdsourcing exploration data from independent pilots, analysing tens of thousands of candidate systems, and planning colonial outposts at the edges of occupied space.

## Phases

### Phase 1: Call for Data (3301-02-23)
Sirius announces plans for colonial outposts and offers monetary rewards for usable exploration data. Junior VP Ana Quin expresses excitement about finally getting board approval.

### Phase 2: Progress Report (3301-02-28)
Eight candidate systems identified. Explorers have limited time remaining to submit data.

### Phase 3: Reconnaissance Complete (3301-03-02)
Hundreds of scout ships complete the survey. Several dozen target sites identified from tens of thousands of candidates.

## Modern Relevance
Sirius's expansionist efforts in 3301 represent the corporate vanguard of human expansion. Their partnership with Universal Cartographics and their reliance on independent CMDRs create a model for future colonisation. These early projects lay groundwork for Sirius's later dominance in logistics and exploration infrastructure.
