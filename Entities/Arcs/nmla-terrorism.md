# NMLA Terrorism

## Summary
The Neo-Marlinist Liberation Army (NMLA) was the most devastating terrorist organisation of the 3300s. Drawing on Marlinist republican ideology—which held that the Empire should be governed by elected representatives rather than hereditary emperors—the NMLA launched a campaign of bombings and assassinations that killed thousands and nearly destabilised the Empire. Their attacks included the Nine Martyrs incident that cancelled the Galactic Summit and the assassination of Prince Harold Duval. The organisation was ultimately destroyed at the Battle of Summerland, but not before revealing that a secret cabal of Imperial senators—the Lords of Restoration—had been funding them.

## Phases

### Phase 1: The Nine Martyrs (3307-03-11)
The NMLA launched simultaneous bombings across nine starports in Alliance, Empire, and Federation space, including the superpower capitals of Achenar, Alioth, and Sol. The terrorist Theta Seven claimed responsibility, broadcasting a manifesto declaring war on the Duval dynasty. Thousands died. The Galactic Summit was cancelled, and inter-superpower trust collapsed. The Marlinist Parliament disavowed the extremists, but suspicion that NMLA agents had hidden among Marlinist refugees poisoned humanitarian efforts.

### Phase 2: Prince Harold's Assassination (3306-10-20)
The NMLA assassinated Prince Harold Duval, a cousin of Emperor Arissa and father of Hadrian Duval. The killing demonstrated the organisation's ability to penetrate Imperial security at the highest levels. Meanwhile, the Empire and Federation blamed each other for the ongoing attacks. Ambassador Luciana accused the Federation of harbouring terrorists, while Vice President Mitchell blamed Shadow President Winters for "laying out the welcome mat." The attacks deepened the superpower rift while the true scope of NMLA infiltration remained hidden.

### Phase 3: The Battle of Summerland (3308-02-24)
The Alliance, Empire, and Federation formed the Affiliated Counter-Terrorism unit (ACT) to coordinate against the NMLA. Investigations revealed that Emperor Arissa had been kidnapped by the Lords of Restoration—a cabal of Imperial senators who were secretly funding the NMLA to justify a coup. The private military firm Darkwater Inc, which had trained NMLA terrorists, made their final stand in the Summerland system. Imperial forces and independent pilots destroyed Darkwater's armada, killing Colonel Ronan Brock. Emperor Arissa declared: "Let the Battle of Summerland mark the victorious end of our bitter war."

## Modern Relevance
The NMLA crisis exposed the fragility of Imperial security and the willingness of powerful figures to fund terrorism for political gain. The Lords of Restoration's conspiracy demonstrated that the Empire's greatest threat came not from external enemies but from its own elite. The formation of ACT established a precedent for inter-superpower security cooperation, though the organisation was later disbanded. The crisis also complicated the status of Marlinist refugees, with legitimate asylum seekers conflated with terrorists—a distinction that remains politically charged.
