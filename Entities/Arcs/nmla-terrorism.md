## Description

The NMLA Terrorism arc traces the escalation of the Neo-Marlinist Liberation Army from fringe extremists to the most lethal terrorist organisation in galactic history. Beginning with small-scale attacks in 3307, the campaign peaked with the Nine Martyrs bombings — simultaneous strikes on nine starports across all three superpowers that killed tens of thousands and demonstrated the NMLA's military-grade capabilities. The group exploited Imperial crackdowns on Marlinist communities to radicalise recruits, while its dark-comms network allowed operatives to evade detection for months.

The aftermath reshaped interstellar security. The Empire's Proactive Detection Bureau authorised mass surveillance of civilian communications, the Affiliated Counter-Terrorism Unit (ACT) was formed to coordinate cross-border operations, and millions of Marlinist refugees fled to the Federation — triggering a humanitarian crisis that nearly sparked superpower war. By mid-3308, ACT had dismantled the NMLA leadership and killed Theta Seven, though the political scars of the terror campaign persist in heightened security measures and lingering Marlinist resentment.
