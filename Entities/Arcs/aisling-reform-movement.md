# Aisling's Reform Movement

## Summary
Aisling Duval's emerging political campaign against Imperial slavery. What begins as an emotional outburst on a reality show quickly coalesces into an organised movement with grassroots support, pitting the young princess against the Empire's traditional power structures.

## Phases

### Phase 1: The Celebrity Pets Outburst (3301-03-06)
Aisling launches a blistering attack on Imperial slavery during a reality show segment, directly naming Senator Torval as an example of the system's cruelty. The Stop Slavery Stupid organisation expands its emancipation centres in response.

### Phase 2: Grassroots Resistance (3301-03-08)
The Future of Synteini party organises a boycott against Torval's slave training facility, calling on pilots to starve Lagerkvist Gateway of business. Anti-slavery sentiment spreads beyond Aisling's celebrity platform into organised political action.

## Modern Relevance
Aisling's reform movement is the first serious challenge to Imperial slavery from within the Imperial family itself. Her combination of media savvy, humanitarian credibility, and royal lineage makes her uniquely positioned to challenge the institution. The movement will grow over the following years, eventually making her one of the most powerful voices in Imperial politics and a persistent thorn in the side of traditionalists like Patreus and Torval.
