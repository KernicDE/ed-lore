# HIP 87621 Conflict

## Summary
The HIP 87621 system, long permit-locked and shrouded in secrecy, became the flashpoint for a major inter-superpower conflict in 3311–3312. The discovery of the unique exobiological organism Radicoida unica within the system triggered a scramble for control between the Federation, Empire, Alliance, and independent powers. The conflict ended with the October Accords, which granted corporate custodianship to the October Consortium—a private entity—while excluding the Federation entirely.

## Phases

### Phase 1: The Lockdown (3311-09-02)
HIP 87621 was a permit-locked system of unknown purpose, with speculation ranging from secret superpower research projects to alien encounters. The situation changed dramatically when exobiologist Terri Tora disappeared in the system and the unique organism Radicoida unica was discovered. This biological treasure—potentially valuable for pharmaceutical and scientific applications—transformed HIP 87621 from a curiosity into a strategic prize.

### Phase 2: The Enclave Conflict (3311-11-27)
Multiple powers moved to establish control over the system, leading to open military conflict. The Federation, Empire, Alliance, and various independent factions all deployed forces. The Holloway Bioscience Institute published promising genetic findings about Radicoida unica, further intensifying competition. Brewer Corporation's Trailblazer fleet became involved, with the Trailblazer Dream in HIP 90578 serving as a staging point for construction efforts in the region.

### Phase 3: The October Accords (3312-02-20)
The October Consortium—originally a tech investor led by the reclusive Lexi October—brokered an agreement ending hostilities. The October Accords placed HIP 87621 under corporate custodianship, with the Consortium acting as neutral chair between Imperial, Alliance, and independent forces. The Federation was notably excluded, having lost its foothold during the conflict's opening stages. The Accords also established shared scientific access to Radicoida unica research.

## Modern Relevance
The HIP 87621 conflict set a significant legal precedent: a private corporation mediating between superpowers and controlling disputed territory. The October Consortium's rise from tech investor to geopolitical broker demonstrated how exobiological discoveries can rapidly reshape power structures. The Federation's exclusion may fuel future reclamation attempts, while the corporate custodianship model could be replicated elsewhere in disputed frontier regions.
