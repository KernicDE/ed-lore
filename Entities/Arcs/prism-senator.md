# Prism Senator Affair

## Summary
Senator Kahina Tijani Loren of the Prism system was one of the more unconventional figures in Imperial politics. In early 3301, she undertook a high-profile tour of Federation space—an unusual move for an Imperial senator—that generated both diplomatic friction and public fascination. Her cryptic statements, refusal to conform to Imperial protocol, and eventual disappearance marked the beginning of a journey that would transform her from a minor senator into the revolutionary figure known as Commander Salomé, with connections to the Children of Raxxla, the Dark Wheel, and Emperor's Dawn.

## Phases

### Phase 1: The Federation Tour (3301-03-06)
Senator Loren began her tour of Federation space, accompanied by a large flotilla and generating significant media attention. She visited Mars High in the Sol system, where her interactions with Federation officials were marked by witty deflection rather than diplomatic formality. When asked if Mars High docking officials were friends or rivals, she replied simply: "Neither." The Alliance also scrutinised her activities, with some questioning whether her tour concealed ulterior motives. Throughout the journey, Loren made statements that seemed designed to provoke rather than reassure.

### Phase 2: Return to Empire (3301-04-02)
After completing her Sol system tour, Loren returned to Imperial space under naval escort, rendezvousing with the Imperial Interdictor Lucius at the boundary of Federation influence. She described the trip as "most enlightening" and joked about navigating "fiendishly complicated Federation protocols." The Empire initially treated her return as routine, but tensions mounted as her behavior grew increasingly erratic. She was summoned to Achenar for questioning regarding her activities.

### Phase 3: Disappearance (3301-05-06)
Senator Loren vanished in the Cemiess system. The Children of Raxxla attempted to destabilise the system's economy to force authorities to reveal her whereabouts. For over a year, her fate remained unknown. When she resurfaced, it was not as a senator but as Commander Salomé—a revolutionary figure linked to multiple underground organisations including Emperor's Dawn, the Phoenix Group, and the Dark Wheel. Her transformation from Imperial politician to anti-Imperial operative remains one of the more enigmatic personal journeys in modern politics.

## Modern Relevance
The Prism Senator affair offers a rare window into how Imperial political figures can become radicalised against the very system they once served. Loren's journey from senator to Salomé suggests that disillusionment with Imperial power structures could convert even privileged insiders into revolutionaries. Her connections to multiple secret societies—the Children of Raxxla, the Dark Wheel, Emperor's Dawn—hint at a network of resistance operating beneath the surface of galactic politics, one that may still be active.
