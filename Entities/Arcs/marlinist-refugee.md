# Marlinist Refugee Crisis

## Summary
The Marlinist Refugee Crisis erupted in late 3306 when hundreds of thousands of believers in Marlinist republican ideology fled the Empire following anti-terrorism lockdowns. The Empire, hunting for Neo-Marlinist Liberation Army terrorists, demanded extradition of all refugees. The Federation, led by Ambassador Jordan Rochester, offered asylum and protection. The Alliance, after initial hesitation, also provided shelter. The crisis tested inter-superpower relations, raised questions about the difference between political refugees and terrorists, and ultimately led to the establishment of Marlinist colonies in Alliance and Federation space.

## Phases

### Phase 1: The Exodus (3306-10-20)
Hundreds of thousands of Marlinists evacuated their homes as the Empire imposed anti-terrorism lockdowns. An Imperial blockade in Ackwada prevented ten megaships from escaping, but others carrying huge numbers of refugees reached Federal systems including Charunder, Ennead, HIP 36081, LTT 1935, LTT 3607, and Thetis. Imperial Ambassador Imogen Luciana demanded extradition, claiming the refugees were connected to NMLA terrorists. Rochester countered that there was no extradition treaty for unconvicted citizens and requested Congress grant asylum.

### Phase 2: Federal Response (3306-10-22)
The Federation launched initiatives to support Marlinist refugees, delivering supplies and establishing temporary accommodation. Prime Minister Edmund Mahon confirmed the Alliance would also welcome refugees, though some independent systems warned they would resist any influx. The crisis escalated when evidence emerged that NMLA activists had concealed themselves among refugee populations, poisoning the humanitarian response with security concerns. Federal agencies began screening refugees for terrorist connections.

### Phase 3: Alliance Shelter (3306-12-24)
Tens of thousands of additional refugees claimed sanctuary in Alliance space. Safeguard Interstellar provided accommodation while Alliance Interpol conducted stringent security checks. The initial wave of Federation-bound refugees prepared to populate independent colonies established by Sirius Corporation. Councillor Nakato Kaine warned of resource limits, arguing the Alliance could not become a "dumping ground for other superpowers' problems." The Marlinist Parliament officially disavowed NMLA extremists while cooperating with security services.

## Modern Relevance
The Marlinist Refugee Crisis established precedents for how superpowers handle mass political migration. The Federation's decision to offer asylum despite Imperial pressure demonstrated its commitment to political freedom principles, while the Alliance's more cautious approach reflected practical resource constraints. The discovery of NMLA agents among refugees also demonstrated how terrorist organisations exploit humanitarian crises, complicating future responses to similar situations. The Marlinist colonies founded during the crisis remain permanent additions to the political landscape.
