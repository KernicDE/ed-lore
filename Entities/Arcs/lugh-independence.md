# Lugh Independence Crisis

## Summary
The Crimson State Group's bid to secure independence for the Lugh system from Federal control. With 87% popular support, they appeal to the Alliance for diplomatic recognition, framing their struggle as one of self-determination against Federal military occupation.

## Phases

### Phase 1: Diplomatic Appeal (3301-02-22)
Ambassador Éamonn Uí Laoghire arrives in Alioth to petition the Alliance for recognition. He addresses crowds of supporters and frames the Federal Navy as the sole obstacle to Lugh's democratic will.

## Modern Relevance
The Lugh crisis is a pivotal test of Alliance-Federation relations and frontier sovereignty. It forces the Alliance to confront whether it will support secessionist movements against the Federation, potentially escalating into superpower conflict. The outcome sets precedents for how independence movements are treated across the galaxy and becomes a touchstone for debates about self-determination versus stability.
