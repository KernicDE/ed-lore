## Biography

The Canonn Interstellar Research Group selected this obscure refinery system for one of its most important projects in March 3302: a state-of-the-art research outpost dedicated to studying Unknown Artefacts. Located well away from major trade routes but close enough to the Pleiades region where alien objects were first discovered, Col 285 Sector IX-T d3-43 offered the isolation necessary for dangerous research combined with reasonable access to source materials.

The campaign to establish the outpost concluded successfully within three months, drawing hundreds of pilots into hauling construction materials across dozens of light-years. By June 3302, the facility was operational, cementing the Canonn's position as the leading independent authority on extraterrestrial artefacts. The outpost remains a testament to what volunteer scientific organisations could achieve when the major powers were still debating whether alien contact warranted serious institutional attention.
