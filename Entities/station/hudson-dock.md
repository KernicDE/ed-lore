## Biography

Hudson Dock is a shipyard in the BD+03 2338 system that has served as a major construction facility for Federal Navy capital ships. In February 3301, Core Dynamics announced a new partnership stage with the Federal Navy to construct several new capital ships, with the first vessel already under construction at Hudson Dock.

The project attracted attention when rumours suggested funding came from an unidentified corporate super PAC — believed to be the same group that had previously helped Shadow President Zachary Hudson gain office. Core Dynamics invited pilots to deliver construction materials to representatives aboard Hudson Dock to assist with the build. The shipyard's role in Federal capital ship production made it a symbol of the military-industrial partnership between the Federal government and Core Dynamics, and its location in BD+03 2338 placed it at the heart of Federal naval expansion during a period of escalating superpower tensions.
