## Biography

In April 3302, Admiral Denton Patreus announced plans to expand the Imperial Navy with a new Majestic-Class Interdictor, selecting Sagan Port in the Mundjiga system as the construction site. Lieutenant Severin oversaw the project and issued an open order for supplies, authorising the Mundjiga Patrons of Law to reward pilots delivering construction materials to the port. The campaign attracted enthusiastic support from loyal Imperial pilots and private military contractors, including the Praetorians and Loren's Legion who defended supply convoys against piracy.

Within a week, the galactic community had delivered thousands of tonnes of essential materials to Sagan Port, enabling the swift completion of the new Interdictor. The successful campaign demonstrated the Empire's ability to mobilise civilian and military resources in pursuit of naval expansion, with Lieutenant Severin thanking supporters on behalf of himself and Admiral Patreus for strengthening the Empire's capacity to meet external threats.
