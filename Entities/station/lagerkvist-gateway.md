## Biography

Lagerkvist Gateway is a starport in the Synteini system that became the focal point of one of the most publicised confrontations over Imperial slavery in the early 3300s. In March 3301, Senator Zemina Torval accepted a challenge from Princess Aisling Duval to determine whose position on slavery the Empire's citizens truly supported, with Torval and the Imperial Society buying unregulated slaves at Lagerkvist Gateway while Aisling's coalition accepted donations of Imperial Slaves for liberation at Mackenzie Relay in Cemiess.

In April 3301, the Imperial Slavers Association confirmed that it had acquired 2,387,567 unregulated slaves — compared to Aisling's Angels freeing 1,149,756 Imperial slaves — and that the unregulated slaves were undergoing ISA-certified retraining at Lagerkvist Gateway. The graduates were expected to go on sale the following weekend. Lagerkvist Gateway's role as the operational centre for Torval's slavery wager made it a symbol of the ideological divide within the Empire over the institution of Imperial slavery, and the station's association with the ISA reinforced its status as a hub for the Empire's most controversial economic practice.
