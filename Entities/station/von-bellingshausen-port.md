## Biography

von Bellingshausen Port is a starport located in the Almar system that fell victim to the technological plague sweeping through inhabited space in January 3302. The station was forced to suspend all services apart from docking and shipyard access after experiencing the mysterious technical malfunctions that would later be linked to Unknown Artefact interference, joining Ackerman Market in Eravate and Haack Gateway in Halbangaay as the latest facilities to be crippled.

The port remained on the list of affected stations for months as the plague spread to dozens of starports across the galaxy, with researchers speculating that meta-alloys might hold the key to reversing the damage. The crisis at von Bellingshausen Port exemplified how rapidly the seemingly inexplicable malfunctions could disrupt trade and travel, foreshadowing the broader Thargoid contact narrative that would reshape galactic security policy.
