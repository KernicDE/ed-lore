# Stillman Hub

Stillman Hub is a starport located in the LTT 1935 system. In March 3307, it was one of nine starports simultaneously bombed by the Neo-Marlinist Liberation Army in the devastating Nine Martyrs attack. The coordinated bombings targeted starports across Alliance, Empire, and Federation space, including superpower capitals. The attack on Stillman Hub and other installations killed thousands and directly led to the cancellation of the Galactic Summit. The NMLA claimed responsibility, broadcasting a manifesto from the terrorist known as Theta Seven.
