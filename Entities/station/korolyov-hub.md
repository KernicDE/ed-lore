## Biography

Korolyov Hub is a starport in the Arawere system that became one of the earliest known victims of the technological plague that swept across inhabited space in late 3301 and early 3302. By January 3302, the station had suspended all services except docking and shipyard access, joining a growing list of affected starports including Gabriel Enterprise in Harma, Gurragchaa Gateway in Warkushanui, and Gaiman Dock in 49 Arietis.

The crisis at Korolyov Hub was eventually traced to Unknown Artefact-related interference, as alien objects transported to the station caused cascading system failures. By February 3302, the technological plague had spread to 22 systems, with Arawere among the most severely affected. The station's experience exemplified the pattern of the early plague wave: initially unexplained technical malfunctions that escalated into full service suspensions, foreshadowing the larger xeno-threat that would dominate the coming decades. Korolyov Hub's recovery, like that of other affected stations, depended on the eventual application of meta-alloys discovered in the Pleiades region.
