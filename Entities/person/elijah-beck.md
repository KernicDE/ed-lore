## Biography

Elijah Beck is a veteran Alliance politician who served in the Alliance Assembly for over four decades before becoming a presidential candidate in 3304. Hailing from the Leesti system, Beck positioned himself as the experienced alternative to incumbent President Gibson Kincaid and entrepreneur Fazia Silva, though observers noted his long record suggested he was unlikely to bring significant change to the presidency.

After Kincaid's impeachment in 3305 for treason, murder and corruption, Beck played a key role in stabilising Alliance governance. He volunteered to resign from the Assembly to oversee the restructuring of the defunct Office of the Alliance President into a non-political ambassadorial department, following the Assembly's historic vote to permanently abolish the presidential office. Beck's career exemplifies the traditional Alliance emphasis on institutional continuity and cautious reform.
