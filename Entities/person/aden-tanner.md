## Biography

Aden Tanner was one of the most experienced naval commanders in the Federation before his appointment as Aegis's chief military liaison in 3303. As Chief of Federal Security, he had overseen counter-intelligence operations across Federal space — experience that proved valuable when the superpowers were forced to cooperate against the Thargoid threat.

In Aegis, Tanner coordinated military responses to incursions, directed defence deployments, and commanded the megaship Musashi. While Tesreau focused on scientific research, Tanner handled the military reality — organising evacuations, defending Pleiades outposts, and planning counter-offensives.

Tanner was among the first senior officers to distrust Salvation, suspecting that the Cornsar "demonstration" had been staged by deliberately luring Thargoids using Guardian artefacts. When official channels failed, he commandeered the Musashi in November 3307 and launched an unauthorised attack on Hind Mine, Salvation's headquarters. Forces loyal to Salvation overwhelmed his task force, and Tanner was detained and court-martialed for mutiny.

The trial was suspended after Ram Tah provided evidence that Salvation's superweapons contained Guardian components derived from the Proteus site — vindicating Tanner's suspicions. Yet the political momentum behind Salvation ensured he remained sidelined and Aegis was dissolved. His career exemplifies the dilemma of honourable officers in dishonourable times.
