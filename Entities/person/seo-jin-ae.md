## Biography

Seo Jin-ae is the sole survivor of Project Seraph, a clandestine Azimuth Biochemicals programme that implanted human test subjects with Thargoid-derived neural interfaces to establish communication with the alien hive mind. Her survival came at an extraordinary cost: the experiments left her with a permanent, involuntary connection to the Thargoid collective consciousness, enabling her to perceive alien intentions and emotional states — a capability that has made her both invaluable and deeply vulnerable.

Rescued by Aegis and later the Far God cult, Seo has become a pivotal figure in humanity's efforts to understand Thargoid behaviour. Her public statements at Aegis symposia provided unprecedented insights into Thargoid psychology, while her meetings with Far God representatives — whom she described with weary frustration as expecting "divine commandments" — demonstrated both her unique access and the burden of being treated as a prophet rather than a scientist. The cult's veneration of her as "the Preceptor" underscores the blurred line between her scientific utility and religious fetishisation.
