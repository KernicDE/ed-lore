## Biography

Captain Saskia Landau is the co-leader of the Affiliated Counter-Terrorism unit who played a pivotal role in dismantling both the NMLA and the secret Imperial conspiracy that had orchestrated its campaign. In February 3308, Landau provided the media with a detailed summary of ACT's investigation into the Lords of Restoration — a cabal of Imperial senators including Gereon, Jourdain, Nerva, Scordato, and Torquatus who had masterminded the NMLA's terrorist activity.

Landau revealed that the Lords had weaponised the NMLA to assassinate the Duvals and restore executive power to the Senate, abducting Emperor Arissa Lavigny-Duval in January 3307 and engineering communications via a virtual doppelganger verified by corrupt Imperial Intelligence agents. She also exposed how Marlinism was exploited as a political lever to make a senator-led Empire appear as a "moderate compromise." Landau had previously warned during the Battle of Mudhrid in November 3307 that Theta Seven's escape would perpetuate the threat of atrocities like the Nine Martyrs bombings. Her leadership in ACT demonstrated how tri-superpower cooperation could unravel even the most deeply entrenched conspiracies.
