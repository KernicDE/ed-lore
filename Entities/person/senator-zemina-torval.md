# Senator Zemina Torval

## Role
Imperial Senator and major slave-holder. Partners with Senator Patreus in business arrangements involving Imperial Slave transfers and debt restructuring.

## Character
Torval is introduced as a pragmatic power broker with extensive interests in the Imperial Slave trade. Her willingness to enter business arrangements with Patreus suggests a shared vision of Imperial expansion through economic means.

## Timeline of Significance
- **3301-02-16:** Business arrangement with Patreus facilitates transfer of 12,070 Imperial Slaves from Durius

## Key Relationships
- **Senator Denton Patreus** — Business partner in slave-trade operations

## Modern Status (3312)
Torval's slave-holding interests make her a polarising figure. While traditionalists respect her as a defender of Imperial customs, abolitionists — including Aisling Duval — view her as emblematic of the Empire's most oppressive practices. Her early alliance with Patreus proves strategically significant.
