# President Halsey

## Role
President of the Federation in 3301. Leads an administration marked by active military intervention in frontier systems and controversial political appointments.

## Character
Halsey presents herself as a decisive leader willing to use military force to "liberate" oppressed populations and crush criminal organisations. Her rhetoric is moralistic and uncompromising. However, her administration is also characterised by internal distractions — notably the death of Vice President Smeaton and the search for a replacement.

## Timeline of Significance
- **3301-02-15:** Referenced by Admiral Vincent as distracted by VP replacement
- **3301-02-17:** Appoints Congressman Ethan Naylor as new Vice President
- **3301-02-20:** Orders all-out assault on Nijotec, expanding Federal military operations

## Key Policies
- Military intervention in Banki, BD+03 2338, and Nijotec
- Frontier representation via Naylor's VP appointment
- Zero-tolerance for "tin-pot dictators" and criminal syndicates

## Modern Status (3312)
Halsey's aggressive foreign policy and tendency toward military solutions create lasting tensions. Her presidency sets the stage for the political fractures that will later bring Zachary Hudson to power. Frontier systems remember her interventions as either necessary liberation or imperialist overreach, depending on their allegiance.
