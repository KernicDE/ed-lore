## Biography

Cardinal Hieronymous is the leader of the Church of Eternal Void, a fringe religious movement that venerates the Guardians as divine protectors against the Thargoid threat. In July 3304, he declared war on the Far God cult — a rival sect that worships the Thargoids — vowing to "expunge their poisonous creed and purge them from the galaxy."

The Cardinal's declaration triggered a wave of violence against Far God worshippers across multiple systems, with Church members firebombing hive-chapels and compelling citizens to attack cultists. Hieronymous publicly praised these attacks as "the Guardians' righteous work," while the death toll among Far God adherents climbed into the hundreds. The Church's illegal incitement led to Hieronymous's arrest in late July 3304, when authorities declared the Church of Eternal Void illegal in every system where it operated. His case remains a significant example of how wartime paranoia can escalate into state-sanctioned religious persecution.
