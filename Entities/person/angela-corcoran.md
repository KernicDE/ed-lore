## Biography

Angela Corcoran is the Deputy Prime Minister of the Alliance who has served as a key figure in Alliance governance during periods of intense political controversy. In August 3307, she addressed the emergence of Onionhead gamma strain, stating that the Alliance deferred to local authorities on narcotics legislation rather than imposing superpower-wide bans like the Federation and Empire. Her measured response reflected the Alliance's traditional emphasis on local autonomy over centralised moral policing.

Corcoran's most consequential act came during the January 3308 vote on the Alliance-Sirius Corporation defence pact, when she abstained rather than endorsing the controversial military alliance. Her refusal to elaborate on her reasons publicly signalled significant unease within the Alliance leadership about merging commercial interests with military command — unease that was shared by Admiral George Varma and Councillor Nakato Kaine. Corcoran's career illustrates the delicate balancing act required of Alliance officials navigating between expansionist ambition and institutional caution.
