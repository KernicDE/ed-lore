# Aaron Salazar

Aaron Salazar served as Chief Financial Officer at Rackham Capital Investments from the company's founding. In March 3305, he suffered a fatal cardiac arrest at the Ivystone Spa. His sudden death created a power vacuum at the multi-billion credit investment firm. The board of directors voted to install Taja Gavaris as his replacement while CEO Zachary Rackham took a leave of absence to cope with his grief. Journalist Bryanna Blanco later suggested Salazar's death was the first in a series of fatal "accidents" targeting Rackham's loyal supporters, part of Gavaris's scheme to seize control of the company.
