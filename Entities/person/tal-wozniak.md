Tal Woźniak served as Security Chief for United Carnoeck for Equality, an organisation operating within Alliance space during the mid-3300s. In this capacity, he coordinated defensive operations against pirate groups threatening local trade routes and civilian installations. His role placed him at the intersection of local security concerns and the broader Alliance Defence Force's strategic priorities, requiring him to balance community autonomy with interstellar military cooperation.

During a notable operation in mid-3304, Woźniak oversaw a campaign to recover stolen data from pirate forces. Following its successful conclusion, he publicly thanked participating pilots and confirmed that the recovered intelligence would enable authorities to track down the responsible criminal group. His leadership demonstrated how security officials in Alliance systems relied on independent pilots to supplement formal law enforcement capabilities, a recurring pattern throughout Alliance frontier governance.
