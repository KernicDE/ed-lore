Commander Raijiin established a reputation as a skilled independent pilot active within the Candy Crew Guild, an organisation known for its distinctive approach to both commerce and community engagement. During late 3302 and 3303, Raijiin participated in the Kick The Alien Buckyball race, one of the more unusual competitive events organised by the Buckyball Racing Club. This race challenged pilots to navigate through Thargoid-influenced territories at maximum speed, combining the technical demands of precision flying with the additional pressure of operating in potentially hostile space.

Raijiin's performance in the race placed him among the top competitors, trailing only Commander Nookie Mr.Smith and ahead of Commander Rewision at the midpoint. This achievement demonstrated both exceptional piloting skill and the courage to push personal and ship limits in environments where conventional wisdom would counsel caution. Through participation in such events, Raijiin exemplified the competitive spirit that drove many independent pilots to test their abilities against peers in increasingly challenging conditions.
