## Biography

Denton Patreus is an Imperial Senator who commands one of the largest private military forces in the Empire, including multiple Majestic-class Interdictors and extensive ground assets. His power base is centred in the Eotienses system, where he maintains estates and a network of client families loyal to his house.

Patreus was already a significant figure in the Imperial Senate when the succession crisis of 3301 thrust him into the centre of galactic attention. On 5 August 3301, Emperor Hengist Duval was assassinated at his wedding ceremony by Brendan Paul Darius — a member of Patreus's entourage who had served for two decades as an officer on the Senator's Majestic-class Interdictors before becoming a patron in his liaison team. Patreus denied any knowledge of the plot and was never formally charged, but the association temporarily devastated his political standing. Mobs attempted to prevent him from paying respects at the Hall of Martyrs, and rivals called for his expulsion from the Senate.

Patreus recovered through decisive military action. When the hardline insurgent group Emperor's Dawn emerged in September 3301, seeking to destabilise the Empire and restore "true" Imperial values, Patreus declared total war on the organisation. He offered generous rewards to independent pilots who aided his campaign and personally directed military operations that destroyed Emperor's Dawn bases across multiple systems. Senator Zemina Torval and Florence Lavigny publicly supported Patreus, with Torval asserting that he was "not an enemy of Hengist Duval." By the time the campaign ended in early 3302, Patreus had effectively eradicated Emperor's Dawn and rehabilitated his reputation.

Patreus's political philosophy combines militarism with pragmatic conservatism. During the succession crisis, he suggested that the Imperial throne should be assigned based on public support rather than lineal descent — a statement widely interpreted as a bid for power. When Arissa Lavigny-Duval was elected Emperor by the Senate, Patreus did not contest the result, but his ambition remains evident. His highly visible courtship of Princess Aisling Duval has led Imperial commentators to speculate that a Patreus-Duval partnership could produce an extremely strong contender for the throne, given that Arissa has no children.

Beyond Imperial politics, Patreus has led military operations against the Kumo Crew pirate syndicate in the Pegasi Sector and consistently advocates for aggressive responses to external threats. He supported the Empire's anti-Thargoid military build-up and has argued for stronger measures against Marlinist dissidents. His combination of military power, political cunning, and dynastic ambition makes him one of the most dangerous figures in Imperial politics.
