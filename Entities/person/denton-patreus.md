## Biography

Denton Patreus commands one of the largest private military forces in the Empire from his power base in the Eotienses system. When Emperor Hengist Duval was assassinated in August 3301 by Brendan Paul Darius — a member of Patreus's entourage — the Senator denied involvement and was never charged. The association temporarily devastated his standing, with rivals calling for his expulsion from the Senate.

Patreus recovered by declaring total war on Emperor's Dawn, the insurgent group responsible for the assassination. Offering generous rewards to independent pilots, he personally directed operations that destroyed Emperor's Dawn bases across multiple systems. By early 3302, he had effectively eradicated the organisation and rehabilitated his reputation.

His political philosophy combines militarism with pragmatic conservatism. During the succession crisis, he suggested the throne should pass by popular support rather than bloodline — widely interpreted as a bid for power. His courtship of Princess Aisling Duval has fuelled speculation about a Patreus-Duval partnership that could produce a strong contender for the throne. Beyond politics, he has led campaigns against the Kumo Crew and consistently advocates aggressive responses to both external threats and internal dissent.
