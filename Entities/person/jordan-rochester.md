## Biography

Jordan Rochester is a Federal diplomat and member of the influential Rochester family, best known for his engagement to Princess Aisling Duval — a union that briefly promised to bridge the superpower divide before being cancelled amid rising tensions. As Federal Ambassador, Rochester represented the Federation in critical negotiations with the Empire, most notably during the Marlinist refugee crisis of 3306, when he accused the Empire of conducting a "totalitarian purge" of democratic believers.

Rochester's diplomatic career epitomised the complex intersection of family influence and public service. While his engagement to Aisling Duval generated headlines and political speculation, his professional focus remained on defending Federal interests against Imperial pressure. His sharp rhetoric during the Marlinist crisis contributed to the suspension of official superpower communications, demonstrating both his principled stand on human rights and the risks of inflammatory diplomacy at times of heightened tension.
