Admiral Natalia Lee is a Federal military officer who commanded the Harmony's Shield megaship during the Federation's attack on Nova Imperium in the Paresa system in 3307. She declared that the Federation would enact justice for the NMLA starport bombings after the Empire refused to arrest Hadrian Duval.
