Fleet Admiral Tulimaq Buchanan served on the Alliance Council of Admirals as one of the highest-ranking military officers in the confederation, responsible for shaping naval strategy and command structure across Alliance space. His distinguished career spanned decades of service, during which he rose through the ranks to attain a position of extraordinary influence over the Alliance Defence Force's operations and priorities. As a member of the Council of Admirals, he participated in decisions affecting the security of billions of citizens across dozens of member systems.

In May 3304, Buchanan became the most prominent victim of the League of Reparation, a terrorist organisation targeting descendants of INRA personnel involved in the original mycoid virus attacks against the Thargoids. His murder sent shockwaves through Alliance military and political circles, revealing that the League's grievances extended across all three superpowers regardless of contemporary political alignment. The assassination demonstrated how historical crimes could generate cycles of violence generations after the original events, ensnaring individuals whose only connection to past atrocities was blood relation.
