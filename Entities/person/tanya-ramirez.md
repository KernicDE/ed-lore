## Biography

Senior Agent Tanya Ramirez is an officer of the Federal Intelligence Agency who served as co-leader of the Affiliated Counter-Terrorism unit (ACT) alongside Captain Niamh Seutonia during the NMLA crisis. In November 3307, Ramirez announced the capture of the NMLA's bomb-making engineers known as Theta Group, though their leader Theta Seven remained at large. She revealed that the breakthrough came from intercepting Theta Group's communications with Neo-Marlinist sympathisers.

Earlier in her career, Ramirez was a member of the tri-superpower taskforce investigating the League of Reparation in 3304, working alongside Alliance Interpol and the Imperial Internal Security Service. Her counter-terrorism expertise spanned two of the most significant threats to galactic security in the 34th century — from INRA-descendant vengeance killings to Neo-Marlinist starport bombings — making her one of the most experienced intelligence officers in the Federation.
