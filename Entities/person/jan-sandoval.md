## Biography

Jan Sandoval is a co-founder of the Red Family, one of the Federation's most notorious narcotics cartels. In June 3305, she unexpectedly surrendered to the Federal Intelligence Agency at its Olympus Village headquarters, offering to testify against the cartel's leadership — including kingpin Oberon Church, to whom she was believed to be second in command.

Executive Agent Viola Trask described Sandoval's surrender as "by far the most notorious" voluntary arrest in FIA history. Sandoval was placed in protective custody, but the cartel responded with a mercenary hit squad that infiltrated FIA headquarters in July 3305 in an assassination attempt. The squad carried false identification to bypass security but was neutralised before reaching Sandoval. Her testimony ultimately provided the intelligence foundation for the FIA's dismantling of the Red Family, making her one of the most consequential informants in Federal narcotics enforcement history.
