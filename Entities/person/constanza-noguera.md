Constanza Noguera served as mayor of a community aligned with Gluscap Labour and the Azaladshu Free movement during the politically turbulent period of mid-3307. Her public statements during this period were notably critical of the Federation, which she characterised as corrupt and authoritarian in its treatment of peripheral systems and independent populations. This rhetoric placed her within a broader current of anti-Federal sentiment that had been building across multiple independent territories frustrated with what they perceived as superpower overreach.

In July 3307, Noguera issued direct calls for supporters to defend local sovereignty, framing the situation as an existential struggle against external domination. Her appeals demonstrated how local officials in independent systems could mobilise resistance to superpower influence by tapping into grievances about taxation, regulatory imposition, and lack of political representation. Noguera's activism illustrated the recurring pattern whereby independent communities sought to preserve autonomy against the gravitational pull of Federal, Imperial, and Alliance expansion.
