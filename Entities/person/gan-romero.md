# Gan Romero

Gan Romero is a former military veteran who became the centre of one of the era's most bizarre criminal mysteries. In April 3305, he stole a Diamondback Explorer from Fort Dixon in the Vega system, bypassing all security protocols and authorisation checks despite having no piloting experience. Station security was at a loss to explain how he gained entry to a docked and sealed ship and flew out unchallenged. The case deepened when investigators discovered Romero had been experiencing vivid dreams that may have driven his actions, raising questions about possible telepathic alien influence.
