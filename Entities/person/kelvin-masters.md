## Biography

Kelvin Masters is a freelance journalist who contributed to the Rewired news feed during the early Thargoid contact period. In 3303, he commented on the Alliance's expansion into the California Nebula, suggesting that superpower competition over barnacle sites would inevitably escalate. Masters also provided some of the earliest public commentary after Professor Ishmael Palin conclusively identified Unknown Ships as Thargoid vessels, warning that historical precedent suggested bloodshed and demanding answers about superpower contingency plans.

His reporting captured the public mood of anxiety and suspicion that characterised the first Thargoid encounters, questioning both the Federation's seizure of Palin's data and the galaxy's preparedness for alien hostility. Masters's contributions to Rewired made him a recognizable voice in the independent media landscape during one of the most consequential periods in recent galactic history.
