# Emil Varga

Emil Varga is a Commodore in the Alliance Defence Force who serves as the Alliance's military liaison to Aegis. In May 3309, he provided a critical tactical briefing following the first pathfinder missions into the Maelstroms. Varga confirmed that independent pilots had encountered significant Thargoid presence in the core regions and warned that all eight Maelstrom systems were affected by the same anti-Guardian field first witnessed in HIP 22460. His advisory that "weapons based on Guardian technology will not function and should be replaced with other anti-xeno armaments" became essential guidance for pilots preparing to enter the caustic clouds.
