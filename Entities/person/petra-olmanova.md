Petra Olmanova established herself as a skilled engineer within Aegis, the tri-superpower organisation created to coordinate humanity's response to the Thargoid threat. Her technical expertise spanned multiple disciplines essential to anti-xeno operations, from weapons development to defensive systems integration. Working within Aegis provided Olmanova with access to resources and research facilities drawn from across Federal, Imperial, and Alliance territories, enabling her to contribute to some of the most advanced military engineering projects of the era.

Her documented career from 3304 through 3309 covered the critical early phases of sustained human-Thargoid conflict, when Aegis struggled to translate scattered encounters into coherent technological responses. Olmanova's engineering work supported the development of weapons and defensive platforms that independent pilots would deploy against alien vessels. Her contributions exemplified the scientific and technical labour that underpinned front-line combat operations, transforming raw research into practical systems capable of engaging a technologically superior adversary.
