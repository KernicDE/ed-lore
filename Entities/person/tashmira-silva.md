## Biography

Tashmira Silva is a corporate executive who assumed control of the Silva corporate empire following the assassination of her sister, Fazia Silva, in October 3304. Fazia had been a candidate in the Alliance presidential race when she was poisoned at Hume Orbital, a murder that remains officially unsolved but was widely suspected to involve political rivals.

Tashmira's most significant political intervention came in mid-3305, when she privately commissioned a Wallglass Investigations Agency report into her sister's death. The report, combined with testimony from Admiral Frederick Yamamoto, exposed President Gibson Kincaid's conspiracy to assassinate Fazia Silva and his broader pattern of treason, murder and corruption. Tashmira's public statement following Kincaid's impeachment — that "the Alliance has been purged of such a corrupt presence" — underscored both her personal loss and her role in exposing one of the most serious constitutional crises in Alliance history.
