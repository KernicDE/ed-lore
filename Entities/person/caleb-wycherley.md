## Biography

Caleb Wycherley was the founder and original director of Azimuth Biochemicals, the corporate predecessor to Azimuth Biotech and the institutional lineage behind the anonymous scientist known as Salvation. In the early 3100s, Wycherley established Azimuth's xenological research division, acquiring the first evidence of Thargoid life and initiating the secret weapons programmes that would eventually produce the mycoid virus in collaboration with INRA.

When Azimuth Biochemicals was commercially overtaken by Pharmasapien over a century later, Wycherley ensured key assets and personnel were removed from company records prior to acquisition, allowing the research to continue in secret. In June 3308, Salvation publicly revealed himself as Wycherley's successor, claiming that "Caleb Wycherley is no more, but his accomplishments laid the foundations for the man I have become." Wycherley's legacy is thus directly tied to both the mycoid virus that ended the First Thargoid War and the Proteus Wave superweapon that catastrophically escalated the Second.
