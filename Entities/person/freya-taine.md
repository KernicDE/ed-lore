# Freya Taine

Freya Taine serves as CEO of Zende Partners, a corporation that expanded into Guardian space with a megaship transport service. In May 3305, Taine publicly accused rival Segnen Exchange of attacking Zende convoys in Synuefe EN-H d11-96, calling it a "flagrant attempt to undermine our efforts." She appealed to independent pilots for support in removing the threat, framing the conflict as necessary to protect Zende's operation and advance production of an Advanced Multi-cannon derived from Guardian technology. The corporate clash in Guardian space set a precedent for how commercial interests could trigger open warfare over access to alien resources.
