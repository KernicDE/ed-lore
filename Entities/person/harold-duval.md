# Harold Duval

Harold Duval was a Prince of the Imperial Duval dynasty and the father of Princess Aisling Duval. Emperor Hengist Duval famously declared him unfit for the throne, after which Harold lived largely out of public view on Capitol. In September 3306, he was making an unpublicised journey to visit Aisling in Cemiess following a recent terrorist attack in that system. A single NMLA assassin infiltrated his personal transport, shooting the Prince at close range before being killed by shipboard guards. The NMLA claimed responsibility, referring to the Duval family as "imperialist parasites" and demanding that Marlinists rise up to demand a democratic republic. How the NMLA learned of his itinerary remains unknown.
