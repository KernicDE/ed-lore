## Biography

Aaron Whyte was a minister in the Marlinist Parliament and a community leader among political refugees fleeing Imperial persecution in late 3306. As head of the Marlinism Reformation Party, he initially advocated for republican ideals within the new colonies. In June 3307, he declared that his faction would politically represent the Neo-Marlinist fringe, causing widespread alarm that the party might act as the NMLA's political wing.

Whyte's overtures toward the Empire and his defence of Neo-Marlinism deepened rifts within the Marlinist Colonies. Following NMLA starport bombings in July 3307, tensions erupted into civil war between his faction and the anti-NMLA Exiled Marlinist Assembly of Baltah'Sine. The conflict ended in August 3307 with the defeat of the Marlinism Reformation Party and Whyte's death, which Minister Octavia Volkov declared justice for the terrorist campaign his collaborators had begun.
