# Shadow President Zachary Hudson

## Role
Shadow President of the Federation — the leader of the opposition party in Federal politics. Former military man with deep connections to the defense industry.

## Character
Hudson is introduced only through association: rumours that a corporate super PAC connected to him is funding Core Dynamics' capital ship construction for the Federal Navy. This suggests he is already building the military-industrial network that will later propel him to the presidency.

## Timeline of Significance
- **3301-02-27:** Rumoured connection to funding for new Federal capital ships via Core Dynamics partnership

## Key Relationships
- **President Halsey** — Political rival; leader of the administration he opposes
- **Core Dynamics** — Alleged financial ties through super PAC

## Modern Status (3312)
Hudson's early connections to defense contractors foreshadow his eventual rise to the presidency. His military background and corporate alliances define his administration, which is marked by aggressive naval expansion and hardline security policies. The seeds of his political machine are visible even in these early rumours.
