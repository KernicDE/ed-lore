## Biography

Professor Ishmael Palin began his career with the Federation's Homeland Scientific Council, leading research into Unknown Artefacts in September 3301. The programme was abruptly terminated after one week under external pressure, prompting Palin to establish the independent Palin Institute at Christian Dock in October 3301. He relocated to Obsidian Orbital in the Maia system by early 3302, issuing appeals for meta-alloy deliveries to counter the technological plague affecting starports.

On 9 February 3302, Palin was kidnapped en route to Maia B1BA by black-clad assailants who interrogated him for days about his research. Discovered a month later in escape pods recovered from the Blue Hand Gang, he described his captors as representing "control, oppression, and the obviation of civil liberties." The ordeal led him to retire from the Palin Institute in April 3302, passing directorship to Professor Cora Shaw, though he continued independent research and established himself as an engineer specialising in thrusters and frame shift drives.

Forced to evacuate Maia by a Thargoid offensive in July 3305, Palin relocated to Abel Laboratory in the Arque system. He remained an active researcher throughout the conflict, collaborating with Ram Tah on hybrid Guardian-Thargoid artefacts in 3308 and announcing a breakthrough in Thargoid spire site analysis in November 3309. His work contributed directly to the Guardian nanite torpedo project in early 3310, making him one of the most consulted scientists in the anti-xeno effort.
