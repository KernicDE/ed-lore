## Biography

Li Yong-Rui is the CEO of Sirius Corporation and architect of many of the megacorp's most consequential political manoeuvres. He brokered the strategic defence pact with the Alliance in 3308, granting Sirius military access in exchange for economic partnerships, and proposed the establishment of independent Marlinist colonies in 3306 to defuse the refugee crisis threatening war between the Empire and Federation. His calm, business-first diplomacy has made Sirius a de facto fourth superpower.

Yong-Rui's leadership has drawn increasing criticism, particularly from Alliance Councillor Nakato Kaine, who accused him of failing to deliver promised capital ships and adequate Thargoid defence. The Alliance Assembly launched a formal evaluation of the Sirius pact in late 3309, questioning whether the partnership's returns justified its costs. Despite this pressure, Yong-Rui has maintained Sirius's influence across all three superpowers, positioning the corporation as an indispensable mediator in galactic affairs.
