## Biography

Captain Niamh Seutonia was an officer of the Imperial Internal Security Service who rose to prominence leading the investigation into the 3304 murder of Senator Nestor Cartesius. She helped uncover the League of Reparation, a terrorist group targeting descendants of INRA members, and served in the tri-superpower taskforce that dismantled it alongside Alliance Interpol and the Federal Intelligence Agency.

In 3307, Seutonia was appointed co-leader of the Affiliated Counter-Terrorism unit (ACT), a tri-superpower agency formed to combat the NMLA after the Nine Martyrs starport bombings. While investigating NMLA suppliers in the Mudhrid system, her ship the Valkyrie went missing. The wreckage was discovered weeks later in the Trianguli sector GW-W B1-0 system, with all crew dead. Seutonia had secured her mission logs moments before the crash, providing vital evidence linking a Neo-Marlinist faction to the bombings. She was posthumously awarded the Achenar Service Cross.
