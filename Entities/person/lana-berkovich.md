## Biography

Lana Berkovich is the Federal Secretary of State who served under President Zachary Hudson during the Thargoid conflicts of the late 3300s. In September 3308, she sponsored a resolution petitioning Congress to make the Order of the Far God illegal, citing constitutional amendments that prevent citizens from providing aid and comfort to an enemy of the state.

Berkovich's resolution followed the Federal Navy Intelligence Office's discovery that the Far God cult was believed to be responsible for leaking classified audiovisual data from the HIP 22460 system. Her hardline stance on Thargoid worshippers aligned with Hudson's broader counter-xeno policy, though it drew condemnation from civil rights organisations including Advocacy and the Galactic Interfaith Commune. Her role in the crackdown exemplified the Federal government's willingness to treat religious dissent as treason during wartime.
