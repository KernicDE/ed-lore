# Taja Gavaris

Taja Gavaris rose from pirate rival to corporate executive in one of the more notorious career transitions in Federal business history. Following the death of Rackham Capital Investments CFO Aaron Salazar in March 3305, Gavaris was installed as his replacement while CEO Zachary Rackham grieved. Federal Times journalist Bryanna Blanco exposed Gavaris's criminal background, revealing she had been a pirate rival of Rackham and used blackmail to force her way into the organisation. Blanco alleged that Gavaris was systematically eliminating Rackham's loyal supporters in a vendetta to seize control. Gavaris ultimately resigned as CFO in April 3305, though the criminal investigation Blanco demanded never materialised.
