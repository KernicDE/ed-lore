## Biography

Yuri Grom is a former Federal Navy admiral who defected from the Federation to establish himself as an independent power. After a brief conflict with his former allies in the Euryale system, he led the EG Pilots to victory in the Dangerous Games of 3302 and founded the autocratic EG Union, ruling his controlled systems with absolute popular support.

In 3307, Grom entered negotiations with the Federation over the Delta Pavonis system, one of the Federation's founding worlds. When undercover Federal agents attempted to apprehend him during the conference, he escaped and declared war on the Federation. EG Union defeated Federal forces and captured the megaship Freedom's Foundation as spoils of war. Grom remains a significant independent power into the 3310s, and in 3312 his forces joined a coalition non-aggression pact in the HIP 87621 enclave alongside Imperial and Alliance representatives.
