## Biography

Tomas Turai was a celebrated actor whose marriage to Core Dynamics executive Jupiter Rochester fifteen years before 3304 made him one of the Federation's most watched celebrity spouses. The union produced two children — daughter Kali and son Kalen — and positioned Turai at the intersection of corporate power and popular culture, a space where the Rochester family's influence over Federal politics met the glamour of the entertainment industry.

The marriage collapsed in 3307 when Jupiter Rochester's corporate scandals became public, prompting Turai to file for immediate divorce and sever all ties with the disgraced executive. In the aftermath, Jupiter's mother Isolde Rochester — a Liberal Party congresswoman — took custody of the two children, while Turai vanished from the public eye. His brief reappearance in galactic news served as a reminder that even the most polished corporate dynasties could unravel when private betrayals became public spectacle.
