# Tomas Turai

Tomas Turai is the patriarch of the wealthy Turai family and father of Jordan Rochester's ex-fiancée. The Turai dynasty is one of the Federation's most influential families, with extensive business interests and political connections. Their planned alliance with the Rochester family through marriage would have united two of the Federation's most powerful dynasties. However, the scandal surrounding Jupiter Rochester's criminal activities—including his conspiracy to destroy Starship One—derailed these plans and cast a shadow over both families. The Turai family's subsequent distance from the Rochesters reflected the political cost of association with disgraced figures.
