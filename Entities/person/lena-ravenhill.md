# Lena Ravenhill

Lena Ravenhill serves as Chief Editor of the Federal Times, one of the Federation's most respected news outlets. In November 3304, she published a bombshell investigative report exposing the spy robot scandal. Ravenhill revealed that an anonymous Achilles Corporation whistleblower had attested to being blackmailed by Kingsley Cordova, owner of the Mars Tribune, into installing surveillance programs on PA912 robots belonging to well-known figures in the Sol system. The accumulated data was relayed to the Cordova Group and published in the Mars Tribune. Her reporting triggered Achilles Corporation's remote kill-switch response and ultimately led to the Mars Tribune's closure.
