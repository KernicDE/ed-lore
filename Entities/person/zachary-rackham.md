## Biography

Zachary Rackham, nicknamed "Calico Zack", is a multi-billionaire businessman and President of the Federation whose past as an independent trader is widely rumoured to have been a cover for piracy. He founded Rackham Capital Investments after a lucrative — and suspiciously unverifiable — trading career, building a corporate empire that attracted intense scrutiny from the Federal Times and Federal Intelligence Agency. In 3305, the sudden death of his longtime CFO Aaron Salazar and the rapid rise of replacement Taja Gavaris triggered an investigation into a string of fatal "accidents" at the company.

Despite these scandals, Rackham's populist appeal and anti-establishment rhetoric carried him to the presidency. His administration has been marked by the same blurring of legitimate business and criminal intrigue that defined his corporate career, with critics continuing to question whether "Calico Zack" ever truly left the pirate life behind.
