# Aisling Duval

## Role
Imperial socialite, humanitarian, and emerging political figure. Daughter of the late Emperor Hengist Duval and cousin to Arissa Lavigny-Duval. Known as "the People's Princess" for her charitable work and opposition to traditional Imperial practices.

## Character
Aisling first appears on GalNet as a humanitarian intervening in the Volungu Blight crisis, bringing Imperial doctors to help afflicted stations regardless of political allegiance. This establishes her as a compassionate figure who transcends faction boundaries — a stark contrast to the hawkish traditionalists like Patreus and Torval.

## Timeline of Significance
- **3301-02-24:** Arrives at Effinger Port with dozens of Imperial doctors and nurses to combat the Volungu Blight
- **3301-02-26:** Her doctors identify the Blight pathogen, enabling the development of a cure

## Key Traits
- Travels with an entourage, referred to as "the Diva Duval"
- Prioritises humanitarian concerns over political boundaries
- Represents a progressive wing of Imperial society

## Modern Status (3312)
Aisling Duval becomes one of the most influential and controversial figures in the Empire. Her early humanitarian interventions evolve into a full political movement advocating for the abolition of Imperial slavery and democratic reforms. She remains a thorn in the side of traditionalists and a beacon of hope for reformers. Her relationship with her cousin Emperor Arissa is famously strained.
