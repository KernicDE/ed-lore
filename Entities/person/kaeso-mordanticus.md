## Biography

Duke Kaeso Mordanticus is the founder and former leader of Nova Imperium, an isolationist movement that challenged Emperor Arissa Lavigny-Duval's legitimacy by advocating for an Empire that severed ties with all foreign powers. In January 3305, Mordanticus was captured during the Battle of Paresa, where Imperial forces destroyed Nova Imperium's fleet.

Chancellor Anders Blaine announced that Mordanticus would be brought before the full Imperial Senate to answer charges of treason — a rare assembly that political observers interpreted as Emperor Arissa's attempt to make a public example of the rebellion's leader. His capture was a severe blow to Nova Imperium, though the movement survived in the Paresa system under new leadership. Mordanticus's trial demonstrated the Empire's determination to crush secessionist movements through show trials rather than quiet disappearances, reflecting the political theatre that sustains Imperial authority.
