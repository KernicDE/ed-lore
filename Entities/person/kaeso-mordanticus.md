# Kaeso Mordanticus

Kaeso Mordanticus is a former Imperial naval admiral who became the leader of Nova Imperium, a radical isolationist faction. A Duke from a long line of decorated military veterans, Mordanticus maintained contacts and influence within the Imperial Navy even after leaving service. An Imperial Internal Security Service leak confirmed that Nova Imperium ships were mostly crewed by ex-naval personnel bound by loyalty to their former commanding officer. Mordanticus's manifesto called for complete Imperial isolation from other superpowers and the installation of Hadrian Duval as Emperor. In January 3305, he was captured after the Battle of Paresa and executed by Senator Denton Patreus on the Imperial Senate floor before Emperor Arissa Lavigny-Duval.
