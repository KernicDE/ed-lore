# Hermine Delarue

Hermine Delarue serves as Executive Operative for Deep Space Surveys (DSS), an organisation with extensive experience supporting large-scale exploration efforts. In January 3312, Delarue issued a public call for mined and manufactured materials to support the Distant Worlds 3 expedition's construction of a new deep-space facility. She cited DSS's prior work on Explorers' Anchorage during Distant Worlds II as precedent and invited independent pilots to help lay foundations for the next era of deep-space discovery. Her leadership in coordinating community-funded frontier infrastructure has established a model for how exploration organisations can crowdsource construction materials.
