## Biography

Jasmina Halsey was elected President in 3300 as a Liberal promising social reform. Her presidency unravelled through a failed Onionhead campaign that firebombed civilian farms and the disastrous War for Lugh, culminating in her order to attack an unarmed refugee convoy — killing approximately 9,322 civilians and collapsing her approval rating to 16%.

On 26 May 3301, Starship One disappeared during a goodwill tour. Presumed dead, Halsey was removed by a Vote of No Confidence that installed Zachary Hudson. She was discovered alive in February 3302, revived from an induced coma with claims of encountering "the caretakers of the galaxy" — super-intelligent beings who showed her "the infinities of the cosmos."

Widely ridiculed, Halsey abandoned politics and relocated to Alliance space, becoming a peace activist and advisor to Prime Minister Edmund Mahon by 3304. In 3307 she testified at Fleet Admiral Vincent's trial for the Starship One conspiracy, subsequently joining the Federal Diplomatic Corps as ambassador to the Alliance.
