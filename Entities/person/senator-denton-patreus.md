# Senator Denton Patreus

## Role
Imperial Senator and prominent power broker. Known for expansionist policies, strategic use of debt-slavery as a tool of assimilation, and hawkish rhetoric on Imperial security.

## Character
Patreus is a commanding orator who frames Imperial expansion as a moral duty. He presents the assimilation of systems like Durius as voluntary and honourable, while his speeches reveal a deep anxiety about threats to Imperial stability. He is pragmatic in forming alliances — notably his business arrangement with Senator Torval.

## Timeline of Significance
- **3301-02-16:** Facilitates transfer of 12,070 Imperial Slaves from Durius via partnership with Senator Torval
- **3301-02-17:** Delivers stirring speech on Eotienses about internal security, references Emperor's failing health, targets Falisci as a threat

## Key Relationships
- **Senator Zemina Torval** — Business and political ally in slave-trade and debt restructuring
- **Emperor Hengist Duval** — Loyal subject; his concern for the Emperor's health is both genuine and politically useful

## Modern Status (3312)
Patreus remains one of the most powerful and controversial figures in Imperial politics. His methods — debt-slavery, military expansion, and aggressive assimilation — are both defended as necessary for Imperial strength and condemned as exploitative. His early alliance with Torval proves enduring and influential.
