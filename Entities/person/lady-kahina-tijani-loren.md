# Lady Kahina Tijani Loren

## Role
Imperial Senator from the Prism system. Known for her strong personality, sharp wit, and willingness to challenge both Imperial and Federal authorities.

## Character
Kahina is charismatic, defiant, and media-savvy. Her confrontation with Mars High docking control — refusing to wait her turn and blocking the docking entrance — establishes her as someone who does not bow to protocol. Her tour of Federal space, complete with an unofficial Imperial Cutter escort, turns diplomatic snubs into public relations victories.

## Timeline of Significance
- **3301-03-06:** Denied docking at Mars High; refuses to move her ship until granted clearance
- **3301-03-09:** Repeat incident at Mars High (duplicate article)
- **3301-03-15:** Receives unofficial Imperial escort through Federal space; quips to Mars Herald: "When I receive one [a welcome], I shall let you know"
- **3301-03-17:** Tours Sol's gas giants; greeted by hundreds of Federal-registered vessels

## Key Quotes
> "As I said before, I don't like to be late."

> "We Imperials often do get accused of being arrogant and overbearing, but at least we're good at it."

## Modern Status (3312)
Lady Kahina Loren remains a popular but politically peripheral figure. Her defiance of protocol entertains the public but limits her access to serious power. She represents a certain type of Imperial aristocrat — proud, independent, and more concerned with personal honour than political advancement.
