## Biography

Vanya Driscoll is a political journalist and correspondent for The Alliance Tribune, specialising in Alliance electoral politics and governance. She rose to prominence during the 3304 presidential election, profiling candidates including entrepreneur Fazia Silva and veteran politician Elijah Beck. Her reporting established her as a leading authority on Alliance political affairs.

Driscoll's most consequential work came in June 3305, when she published a detailed exposé revealing that Admiral Frederick Yamamoto had committed multiple crimes on the direct orders of President Gibson Kincaid, including the assassination of Fazia Silva. The report, based on Yamamoto's plea-bargain testimony, detailed Kincaid's conspiracy to transfer executive power from Alioth to Zaonce and implicated corporate and military figures across the Alliance. Driscoll continued covering Alliance politics through the 3307 prime minister election and the controversial Sirius defence pact debates.
