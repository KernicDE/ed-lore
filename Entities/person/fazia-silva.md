## Biography

Fazia Silva was a successful entrepreneur from the Vamm system who ran for the Alliance presidency in 3304 as a political outsider. Campaigning on her business acumen and proposed financial reforms, she quickly gained support rivalling that of incumbent President Gibson Kincaid and veteran politician Elijah Beck. She delegated control of her corporate empire to her sister Tashmira to focus on the campaign.

On October 5th 3304, Silva was found dead at Hume Orbital, poisoned by a sophisticated nerve toxin administered by professional assassins. The investigation closed without convictions. The following year, Admiral Frederick Yamamoto confessed that he had arranged her assassination on direct orders from President Kincaid, as part of a wider conspiracy to consolidate executive power. Kincaid was subsequently impeached and removed from office, while Tashmira Silva assumed permanent control of the corporate empire her sister had built.
