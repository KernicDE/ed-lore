## Biography

Gethin Okonkwo is a journalist and sociological commentator who has written extensively on the societal impact of the Thargoid conflicts. In 3304, he published a report highlighting the emergence of doomsayer cults in response to the return of the Thargoids, drawing academic attention to the psychological fragility of populations facing existential alien threats. His work was cited by exotheologian Dr Alfred Ulyanov in a subsequent study of the Far God cult — a fringe religion that worships the Thargoids as divine messengers.

Okonkwo's journalism has focused on the margins of galactic society, tracking how fear and uncertainty generate new religious and social movements. His reporting provided an early warning about the spread of Thargoid sympathiser movements and the social conditions that make citizens susceptible to extremist ideologies, establishing him as a respected voice on the human cost of interspecies conflict.
