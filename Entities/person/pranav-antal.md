## Biography

Pranav Antal, known as the Simguru, is the founder and spiritual leader of Utopia, a highly advanced communal society based in the Polevnic system. He advocates for a post-scarcity existence achieved through nanotechnology, neural interfaces, and radical social engineering, positioning Utopia as both a technological pioneer and a philosophical alternative to the superpowers' competitive militarism. His calm, almost mystical public persona masks a shrewd political operator who has successfully kept Utopia independent and influential despite its small size.

Antal's most significant interventions in galactic affairs include the dispute with Vitadyne Labs over salvaged Utopian nanomedicines in 3305, which the Interstellar Health Organisation ultimately ruled in Vitadyne's favour. During the Thargoid crisis, he proposed the Sim-Archive as a means to preserve human consciousness should the species face extinction — a typically Utopian blend of technological optimism and pragmatic contingency planning that drew both admiration and ridicule from the wider galaxy.
