# Prime Minister Edmund Mahon

## Role
Prime Minister of the Alliance of Independent Systems. Leader of the Alliance Assembly and chief architect of Alliance foreign policy.

## Character
Mahon is introduced as a cautious, legally-minded statesman. His refusal to support Lugh — while the Crimson State Group enjoys 87-91% popular support — is framed as adherence to international law rather than moral cowardice. He prioritises stability and non-intervention over ideological alignment, even when the cause aligns with Alliance values (self-determination, democracy).

## Timeline of Significance
- **3301-03-11:** Addresses the Alliance Assembly, warning the Crimson State Group that they stand alone and rejecting any Alliance intervention in Lugh

## Key Policies
- Strict non-intervention in internal affairs of other superpowers
- Rejection of Lugh's application to join the Alliance until full independence is achieved
- Legalistic approach to foreign relations

## Modern Status (3312)
Mahon's cautious leadership defines the Alliance's position between the Empire and Federation. His non-intervention policy preserves Alliance security but limits its moral authority. Under his leadership, the Alliance expands through economic cooperation and terraforming rather than military conquest. He remains Prime Minister for years, becoming one of the longest-serving heads of government in galactic history.
