## Biography

Rear Admiral Riri McAllister was a senior officer in the Alliance Defence Force who was unmasked in July 3304 as "Nexus," the central coordinator of the League of Reparation terrorist group. McAllister had used her position to frame Admiral George Varma with false leads, misdirecting the tri-superpower taskforce investigating the League's murders of INRA descendants. She was arrested and formally charged with conspiracy to commit terrorism following a breakthrough by Deputy Inspector Mara Klatt of Alliance Interpol.

McAllister's betrayal shocked Alliance military leadership and exposed profound security vulnerabilities at the highest levels of the ADF. The decrypted data from her arrest revealed the locations of two major League enclaves, enabling the taskforce to launch operations to eradicate remaining terrorist cells. Her exposure as Nexus remains one of the most disturbing instances of senior military personnel orchestrating mass murder in modern galactic history.
