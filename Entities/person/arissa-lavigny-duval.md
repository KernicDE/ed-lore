## Biography

Arissa Lavigny-Duval was born in 3243 during an affair between Emperor Hengist Duval and Florence Lavigny. Raised as a Lavigny, she became a senator and built a reputation for tackling corruption and championing honour. In December 3300, she publicly declared her true parentage after Hengist decreed his legitimate son Harold unfit to rule.

Following Hengist's assassination by Emperor's Dawn in August 3301, the Senate deliberated for two months before electing Arissa on 6 October 3301. She became the first woman on the Imperial Throne, defeating competing claims from Aisling Duval and Denton Patreus.

Her reign faced immediate existential challenges: Thargoid incursions from 3303, the Marlinist refugee crisis of 3306-3307, and her own abduction by the Lords of Restoration in January 3307 — a conspiracy covered up for over a year using a body double. Arissa's governance combines cautious conservatism with rigorous emphasis on honour and anti-corruption, making her one of the most consequential rulers in Imperial history.
