## Biography

Zachary Hudson was born in 3218 and built his career in the Republican Party, becoming Shadow President during Jasmina Halsey's Liberal administration. When Halsey disappeared aboard Starship One in May 3301, Congress passed a Vote of No Confidence and installed Hudson as President.

His presidency was defined by militarism and corporate alignment. Hudson increased military spending, promoted the Close Quarters Combat championship as a recruitment tool, and declared on Thargoid contact: "If the Thargoids want a fight, it's a fight they'll get." He established the Proactive Detection Bureau to monitor private communications across Federal space.

The defining scandal emerged in 3307, when trials revealed that Admiral Lucas Vincent had conspired with Core Dynamics CEO Jupiter Rochester to destroy Starship One and assassinate Halsey. Though no evidence showed Hudson personally knew of the plot, the association damaged his credibility. He survived a mandatory Vote of No Confidence in 3305 by a narrow margin, but left office in 3310 after Felicia Winters defeated his chosen successor Jerome Archer.
