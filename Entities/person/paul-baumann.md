## Biography

Dr Paul Baumann is the chair of the independent board of inquiry that investigated Aegis's operational failures in 3307–3308. The board was established following the loss of the megaship Alexandria and the assault on Hind Mine, both catastrophes that exposed severe shortcomings in Aegis's management.

The final Baumann Report, published in January 3308, delivered a damning assessment: Aegis had developed a "silo mentality" with departments focusing on themselves rather than strategic goals, relied excessively on "disposable mercenaries" (independent pilots), and failed to produce breakthroughs in anti-xeno technology. The report concluded that Aegis should be dismantled entirely — a recommendation that all three superpowers accepted. Notably, the report did not discuss Salvation or propose alternatives to Aegis, effectively clearing the path for Azimuth Biotech's ascendancy. Baumann's inquiry remains one of the most consequential investigations in modern military history, though critics argue it ignored Azimuth's sabotage of the Alexandria.
