## Biography

Senator Zemina Torval is one of the most powerful traditionalists in the Imperial Senate, controlling shareholder of Mastopolos Mining and a major shareholder in the East India Company. She is a fierce defender of Imperial slavery, famously defeating Princess Aisling Duval in the 3301 "Great Slaver Showdown" by training and "providing for" over two million non-citizens through Imperial Slavers Association facilities in Synteini. Her business empire extends across mining, trade, and narcotics, making her one of the wealthiest individuals in the Empire.

Torval navigated the 3301 succession crisis with characteristic pragmatism, initially defending Denton Patreus against accusations linking him to Emperor Hengist's assassin, then backing Patreus's campaign against Emperor's Dawn, and finally shifting her support to Arissa Lavigny-Duval — a move that effectively sealed Arissa's path to the throne. She commands a substantial private fleet and is widely regarded as the matriarch of a developing dynasty, framing her ambition as dynastic responsibility rather than personal greed.
