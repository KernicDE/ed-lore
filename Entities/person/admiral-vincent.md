# Admiral Vincent

## Role
Senior commander in the Federal Navy, spokesperson for military operations during the Banki and BD+03 2338 rebellions.

## Character
Vincent projects confidence and decisiveness. His public statements emphasise swift, overwhelming force and zero tolerance for "terrorist rebels." He is unapologetic about Federal military expansion into frontier territories.

## Timeline of Significance
- **3301-02-15:** Declares Banki and BD+03 2338 rebellions will be "over by breakfast"
- **3301-02-20:** Announces intention to pursue Polahukuna Raiders into their home system

## Key Statements
> "All foreign, and indeed any domestically grown, terrorist rebels we find will be dealt with decisively."

> "The Polahukuna Raiders, and those like them, need to learn that their actions have consequences."

## Modern Status (3312)
Vincent's hawkish doctrine becomes embedded in Federal military culture. His approach to frontier rebellions — swift retaliation and pursuit into enemy territory — is cited both as a model of effective counter-insurgency and as an example of Federal overreach that fuels anti-Federal sentiment in independent systems.
