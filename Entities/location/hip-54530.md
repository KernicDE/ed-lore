# HIP 54530

HIP 54530 is a densely populated Federal system with nearly half a billion inhabitants and an industrial high-tech economy. The system gained notoriety in 3307 when it became the headquarters of Jupiter Division, a breakaway corporate faction led by Core Dynamics CEO Jupiter Rochester. Rochester attempted to use the system as a base for his corporate rebellion, but Federal forces intervened. The system's high security and massive population made it a strategic prize in the corporate-political conflict that ultimately led to Rochester's downfall.
