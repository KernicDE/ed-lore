## Biography

Turner's World is a planet in the Alioth system, one of the most politically significant systems in the Alliance. In June 3304, the planet's Garden City Gallery was the scene of a high-profile art theft in which a valuable digital painting titled *Youscape* was stolen from a titanium vault protected by plasma grids and thermal sensors. The thief left a spray-painted graphic of a winking cat in its place, baffling security experts.

Later that month, Turner's World became a flashpoint in the conflict with the League of Reparation when Chief Inspector Kay Kilbride of Alliance Interpol was murdered there along with four fellow agents. Kilbride had been lured to an abandoned foundry in New Rossyth by false intelligence, where the entire complex exploded — a calculated act of retaliation by the terrorist organisation for her recent successes against them. The planet's association with both sophisticated criminal activity and the League of Reparation's deadliest strike made it a symbol of the era's rising instability.
