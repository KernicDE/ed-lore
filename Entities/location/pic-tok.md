## Biography

Pic Tok is an independent system specialising in extraction, located in the core regions of inhabited space. In early 3302, the system was among those affected by the technological plague, a mysterious wave of starport malfunctions later linked to Unknown Artefact interference. Lobachevsky Outpost in Pic Tok was forced to suspend all services except docking and shipyard access during the crisis.

The system's experience mirrored that of dozens of others across inhabited space, contributing to the growing realisation that alien technology was disrupting human infrastructure. Like other affected locations, Pic Tok's facilities were eventually restored through the application of meta-alloys harvested from barnacle sites in the Pleiades sector.
