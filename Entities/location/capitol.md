## Biography

Capitol is the capital world of the Empire, a sprawling urban planet serving as the political and military heart of Imperial civilisation. The city was founded by Marlin Duval's colonising fleet and transformed by her brother Henson into the seat of Imperial power. Its surface is dominated by the Imperial Palace — a vast structure of pink glass-like material that shimmers in the sunlight.

The Palace complex contains the Emperor's residences, the Senate chamber, the Hall of Martyrs, and administrative offices. The city extends outward in concentric rings, with nobles in the inner districts and lower classes in the outer rings — a physical manifestation of Imperial hierarchy. Capitol has witnessed many of the Empire's most dramatic moments: Hengist Duval's assassination at his wedding in August 3301, his state funeral in September, and Arissa Lavigny-Duval's coronation in October.

With over 16 billion inhabitants, Capitol is one of the most densely populated worlds in the Empire. Security is always heavy, with drastic increases during crises such as the Emperor's Dawn insurgency and the NMLA terror campaign.
