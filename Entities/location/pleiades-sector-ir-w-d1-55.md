## Biography

Pleiades Sector IR-W d1-55 is a system in the Pleiades Nebula that became a pivotal site in humanity's early response to the Thargoids. In July 3303, the Ocellus starport The Oracle relocated to the system to establish a dedicated research centre at a Thargoid structure site, with Governor Ahmed stating the facility would help answer fundamental questions about Thargoid intentions and communication.

The system's significance escalated dramatically in September 3303, when Thargoid forces attacked Federal ships in Pleiades Sector IR-W d1-55. The incident prompted Aegis — the tri-superpower research initiative — to widen its remit to include defence as well as research, with its budget dramatically increased and sanctions granted by all three superpower leaders. The system thus marks a critical turning point where scientific curiosity gave way to militarised response in humanity's encounter with the Thargoids.
