## Biography

Gateway is a heavily populated Alliance system governed corporately under the Alliance Office of Statistics, with a refinery-based economy supported by agricultural output. Its population of over 400 million makes it one of the most significant Alliance systems outside the core Old Worlds cluster.

The system rose to galactic prominence in 3307 as the logistics hub for Prime Minister Edmund Mahon's re-election expansion programme. Pilots delivered industrial materials and mined tritium to Dublin Citadel in Gateway to fund construction of new Alliance starports in LHS 2522 and the Sharru Sector. The campaign became controversial when Councillor Nakato Kaine revealed irregularities and alleged that Sirius Corporation had made unregistered donations to cover shortfalls — foreshadowing later concerns about corporate influence over Alliance policy.

Gateway is also home to Gateway Laboratories, a major xenological research facility. In 3309, Dr Maximo Fonseca of Gateway Laboratories took the lead on Aegis's Thargoid research, contributing to the development of the caustic sink launcher that enabled pilots to survive longer inside Maelstrom caustic clouds. The system's dual role as both a political campaign hub and a scientific research centre underscores its strategic importance to the Alliance.
