## Biography

LFT 824's four-figure population lived under the Sidewinder Syndicate until July 3307, when the Azaladshu Free faction's successful secession from the Federation transformed the system into an independent democracy. The rebellion spread to LFT 824, LHS 277, LTT 3919 and the Azaladshu system itself, triggered by opposition to the Proactive Detection Bureau's monitoring of private communications.

President Hudson denounced the secessionists as criminals and ordered Federal forces to retake the lost systems. LFT 824's fate became entangled in the broader constitutional crisis, with Shadow President Winters denying she had orchestrated the rebellions while conceding that the surveillance laws were unconstitutional. The tiny system's defiance exemplified how even marginal populations could challenge superpower authority.
