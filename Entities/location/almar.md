## Biography

Barely more than a thousand people called Almar home when the technological plague reached it in January 3302. The Empire's corporate extraction outpost, controlled by Leunii Allied PLC, offered minimal services even at the best of times — all the more reason why the sudden suspension of commodities markets, rearmament facilities and ship outfitting hit hard.

The system was part of a cluster that included Eravate, Halbangaay and others, all suffering identical malfunctions traced to proximity with unknown artefacts. For Almar's tiny population, the crisis underscored how even the most marginal settlements could not escape the cascading effects of alien technology. Recovery depended on the same meta-alloy treatments being pioneered at stations like Ackerman Market, linking the fate of this forgotten outpost to events unfolding hundreds of light-years away.
