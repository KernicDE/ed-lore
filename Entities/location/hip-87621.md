## Biography

HIP 87621 is a permit-locked system in independent space that became one of the most controversial scientific zones of the 3310s. Controlled by the October Consortium, the system was closed to independent access for over a decade under the guise of a legitimate planetary research initiative. In 3311, long-range scans revealed anomalous exobiological readings, prompting billionaire Zachary Rackham to fund a galaxy-wide exploration campaign to cross-reference the signatures.

The mystery deepened when it was confirmed that colonisation had been blocked within a 37-light-year radius of the system. In November 3311, the Pilots' Federation brokered exclusive access for licensed Commanders, who discovered Radicoida unica — an exobiological flora unlike any previously catalogued. October Consortium officials dismissed inconsistencies in system data as "spoofing" and permanently banned fleet carriers. The system later became a flashpoint for superpower conflict, with Alliance, Imperial, and Federal forces all deploying to the HIP 87621 enclave.
