## Biography

Alioth's history is defined by resistance. Colonised in the early years of interstellar expansion, the system became a centre of the hydrogen fuel trade by 2530. In the mid-26th century, the "Caker" rebellion emerged on the moon Fruitcake, launching attacks against Imperial and Federal occupation. The superpowers ultimately cooperated to crush the uprising in 2618, partitioning the system — Fruitcake was renamed Gordonworld under Federal control, while Imperial forces seized local space facilities.

The turning point came in 3228, when Federal corporations attempted to raise prices in New California, triggering a full-scale revolt. Mic Turner and Meredith Argent led fighters from Alioth and neighbouring systems to repel both superpowers. In 3230, the Alliance of Independent Systems was formally established on Alioth, with over twenty systems joining within two decades.

Today, Alioth remains the political and economic heart of the Alliance. The permit-locked system hosts the Alliance Assembly, the Prime Minister's residence, and the Council of Admirals. In February 3307, Lakon Spaceways relocated its headquarters here after the Alliance purchased a majority sharehold. The following year, Sirius Corporation established a permanent presence under the controversial strategic defence pact — a move criticised by pro-independence factions who argue it undermines Alliance sovereignty.
