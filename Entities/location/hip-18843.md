In early 3302, the system was drawn into the technological plague crisis when Alexander Freeport began experiencing the mysterious malfunctions that were disabling starports across inhabited space.

While some starports were eventually restored through meta-alloy treatments, Alexander Freeport in HIP 18843 remained among those experiencing persistent technical issues, underscoring both the severity of the crisis and the uneven success of early countermeasures.
