## Biography

Earth is the birthplace of humanity and the symbolic heart of the Federation, located in the Sol system. As the cradle of human civilisation, it has remained a centre of political, cultural and historical significance across millennia of spacefaring expansion. The planet has hosted key diplomatic events, including receptions for Imperial Senator Kahina Tijani Loren at Cambridge spaceport and in London, and serves as an administrative anchor for Federal governance.

During the 3310 Thargoid invasion, Earth became the focal point of the Second Thargoid War when Titan Cocijo assaulted the Sol system. Mass panic triggered the evacuation of over 22 billion people from Earth, Mars and major ports, with Federal military and freelance pilots conducting desperate rescue operations. Following Titan Cocijo's destruction in December 3310, its wreckage above Earth drew thousands of visitors and a memorial beacon was deployed to honour those lost in the conflict.
