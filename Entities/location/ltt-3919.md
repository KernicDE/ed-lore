## Biography

LTT 3919 is an independent corporate system with a refinery and industrial economy, controlled by the Star Pilots faction. In June 3307, the system was among four controlled by Azaladshu Free that declared independence from the Federation in protest against the Proactive Detection Bureau's mass civilian surveillance programme.

The secession transformed LTT 3919, alongside Azaladshu, LFT 824 and LHS 277, into independent systems, triggering a military response from the Federation. President Zachary Hudson ordered Neche Inc to engage the secessionists and deployed the megaship Fortress Liberty to the region, while Azaladshu Free's victory against Federal forces inspired additional factions including the Coalition of Uteran, Lp 726-6 Free and Gluscap Labour to follow suit.
