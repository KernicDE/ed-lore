## Biography

Sirius Corporation CEO Li Yong-Rui identified HIP 8396 as a priority for economic revitalisation in March 3301, part of a broader strategy to develop systems brought under corporate control during the company's recent expansion. With barely four hundred thousand inhabitants and an extraction-based economy managed by the Selous Syndicate, the system represented exactly the kind of underdeveloped frontier territory where Sirius Gov could demonstrate its developmental model.

The revitalisation campaign called for osmium deliveries to jump-start industrial expansion and financial recovery. Though distant from the major trade routes — sitting over two hundred light-years from Sol in a direction rarely travelled by commercial freight — HIP 8396 attracted enough pilot participation to meet initial targets. The programme established a template Sirius Corporation would replicate across dozens of systems, using resource drives to transform marginal extraction economies into integrated corporate assets.
