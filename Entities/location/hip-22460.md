## Biography

HIP 22460 was an obscure system until it became the site of the most consequential battle of the Second Thargoid War. Located far from the Core Systems, its remoteness made it attractive to Azimuth Biochemicals, which established secret research facilities there decades before the main conflict.

In 3307–3308, independent pilots discovered abandoned Azimuth facilities in HIP 22460 and the nearby HIP 26176 system. These sites were identified as the locations of Project Seraph — a programme of horrific human experimentation in which Azimuth scientists attempted to install human pilots into captured Thargoid scouts. The project logs referenced both "The Witch" and Azimuth, providing early evidence that the supposedly defunct corporation had survived into the 34th century. These discoveries were crucial in building the case against Salvation, though the superpowers initially ignored the implications.

The system's defining moment came in August 3308. Salvation had constructed a massive Guardian-derived superweapon — the Proteus Wave — based on technology recovered from the Proteus wreckage. The weapon was installed aboard the Bright Sentinel megaship and activated in HIP 22460, where a significant Thargoid presence had been detected. The Proteus Wave emitted an electromagnetic pulse designed to disrupt Thargoid biomechanical vessels across the entire system.

Initially, the weapon appeared to work. Thargoid vessels were disabled and destroyed in large numbers. But the activation triggered a catastrophic response. The Thargoids deployed Titan-class vessels — previously unknown megaships of immense scale — and unleashed a counter-wave that overwhelmed human defences. The Bright Sentinel was destroyed, and Salvation (Dr. Caleb Wycherley) was killed, his body later found frozen on the megaship's bridge.

The Battle of HIP 22460 transformed the Second Thargoid War. The deployment of Titan-class vessels and the scale of the Thargoid counter-attack demonstrated that humanity had gravely underestimated its opponent. The battle prompted a radical reassessment of anti-xeno strategy across all three superpowers and marked the end of the Salvation era — though the consequences of his provocation would continue to unfold for years.
