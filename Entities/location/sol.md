## Biography

Sol is the star around which human civilisation originated, and every coordinate in human space is measured relative to its position. Human expansion began tentatively in the 2070s with Moon bases and the first Mars landing. In 2100, the discovery of ancient Martian fossils and confirmation of hyperspace existence reshaped humanity's future, leading to Frame Shift Drive technology and interstellar probes by the 2130s.

The Federation was formally established in 2242, unifying Earth and its colonies. Sol's political dominance ended in 2325 after the defeat at the Battle of Achenar, prompting the Federal capital's relocation to Mars. By the 34th century, Sol's political importance has diminished relative to systems like Achenar and Alioth, yet it remains one of the most heavily populated systems in human space. Earth has recovered from centuries of ecological damage, while Mars hosts the Federal Congress. During the Thargoid conflicts, Sol was heavily fortified and has never fallen to external attack.
