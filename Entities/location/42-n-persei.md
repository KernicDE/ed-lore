## Biography

42 n Persei is an independent system with a population of approximately 1.2 million, controlled by the Anti Xeno Initiative under a patronage government. Located near the frontier of human-Thargoid contact zones, the system's extraction and military economy reflected its position on the edge of contested space.

In September 3308, 42 n Persei was one of three inhabited systems — alongside Chun Pindit and Tekkeitjal — subjected to a coordinated Thargoid invasion, the first such incursion into populated territory since the Proteus Wave incident. Over seven million people were threatened across the three systems, and while no starports were directly attacked, civilians evacuated en masse fearing caustic enzyme bombardment. The invasion demonstrated a tactical evolution in Thargoid strategy, expanding the war beyond the HIP 22460 conflict zone into populated territories. 42 n Persei's experience proved that even independent systems far from the major invasion fronts could not consider themselves safe from Thargoid incursion.
