# Wells-class Carrier

The Wells-class Carrier is a massive mobile fleet carrier design operated by the Federation. These vessels are capable of travelling between star systems and serve as mobile bases for squadrons of fighters and smaller ships. Unlike stationary installations, Wells-class carriers can reposition to support operations across vast distances, making them invaluable for extended military campaigns. Their deployment has been particularly significant in anti-xeno operations, where the ability to establish forward operating bases in threatened systems provides a tactical advantage against Thargoid forces.
