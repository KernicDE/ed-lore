# Imperial Clipper

The Imperial Clipper is a prestigious medium-sized vessel manufactured by Gutamaya exclusively for the Empire. Available only to pilots who have achieved the rank of Baron or higher in the Imperial Navy, the Clipper combines elegant aesthetics with impressive speed and cargo capacity. Its sleek, curved design has made it an iconic symbol of Imperial luxury and status. While its manoeuvrability and firepower are balanced by relatively weak shields for its size, the Clipper remains a popular choice for traders, couriers, and pilots who value both performance and prestige.
