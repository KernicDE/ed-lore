The FNS Nevermore stands as a Farragut-class Battlecruiser, one of the most powerful vessels in the Federal Navy's inventory, constructed through a collaboration between Core Dynamics and Federal military engineers. These massive capital ships serve as fleet command centres, mobile shipyards, and overwhelming combat platforms capable of projecting Federal power across entire sectors. The Nevermore's commissioning represented a significant investment in Federal naval capability during a period of intensifying superpower competition.

In March 3301, the Nevermore became embroiled in the rapidly escalating conflict surrounding the Lugh system when President Jasmina Halsey's declaration of war diverted the vessel from its planned offensive against Polahukuna. This last-minute redirection illustrated the chaotic nature of Federal military decision-making during the crisis, as strategic priorities shifted in response to political developments. The Nevermore's diversion highlighted how even the most powerful naval assets could be subject to abrupt reallocations driven by executive orders rather than operational planning.
