## Biography

Onionhead gamma strain, commonly known as 'helix', is a genetically modified variant of the onionhead narcotic developed for mass production and wider distribution. Unlike the alpha, beta and Lucan strains, which rely on naturally grown seeds from the Panem system, the gamma strain uses synthetic molecules that reduce hallucinogenic intensity while enabling artificial cultivation and longer storage life.

The Interstellar Health Organisation confirmed in 3307 that the gamma strain was engineered for commercial scalability — a sophistication that suggested development by an unidentified research facility rather than the nomadic Blue Viper Club who initially distributed it. The Kumo Crew syndicate under Archon Delaine subsequently mass-produced helix through a partnership with the Club, prompting system-wide crackdowns. The gamma strain represents a troubling evolution in narcotics trafficking: a designer drug engineered by advanced corporate laboratories and distributed through pirate networks.
