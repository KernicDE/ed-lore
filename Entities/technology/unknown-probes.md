# Unknown Probes

Unknown Probes are alien objects closely related to Unknown Artefacts, representing another facet of Thargoid reconnaissance technology. When scanned with an advanced discovery scanner, a probe emits an electro-magnetic pulse that temporarily incapacitates the scanning vessel. This is followed by a burst of sound similar to the "chittering" produced by Unknown Artefacts. Canonn Interstellar Research Group scientist Commander Rizal72 discovered that these sound recordings contain an encrypted image interpreted as a depiction of interstellar bodies. The exact meaning of the image and the probes' ultimate purpose remain unclear, though their similarity to Unknown Artefacts suggests they serve a complementary scouting function in Thargoid intelligence gathering.
