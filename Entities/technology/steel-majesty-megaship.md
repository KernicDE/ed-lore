The Steel Majesty served as the mobile headquarters and refuge for the Neo-Marlinist Order of Mudhrid, a megaship that became synonymous with the faction's survival during the intense crackdowns of 3307. When Imperial forces moved to dismantle the Order following its exposure as a funding source for the NMLA terrorist network, Landgrave Arastin Delacroix evaded capture by fleeing aboard this vessel. The megaship's mobility and defensive capabilities made it a formidable sanctuary, capable of resisting conventional interdiction efforts while sustaining a substantial population of political refugees and loyalists.

Following the Order's military defeat, survivors arrived in the Marlinist Colonies aboard the Steel Majesty, requesting asylum from authorities who faced the difficult decision of whether to harbour remnants of an organisation linked to terrorist activities. The vessel's journey from military command centre to refugee transport illustrated the rapid reversals of fortune that characterised the Marlinist political movement. The Steel Majesty's story demonstrated how megaship technology could serve as both an instrument of insurgency and a lifeline for displaced populations.
