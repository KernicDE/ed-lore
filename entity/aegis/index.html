The goal is to destroy Thargoid vessels and deliver Bio-Mechanical Conduits, Propulsion Elements and Weapon Parts to Dantec Enterprise for analysis. Aegis Core appealed to independent pilots to support the operation.
3304-03-10
Thargoid Surveillance in the Pleiades
Aegis has launched the Eagle Eye initiative to monitor Thargoid activity in the Pleiades Nebula. Six orbital surveillance installations have been established to measure energy-level fluctuations and track ship movements, each attended by a comms beacon. The installations are in HIP 17225, HIP 17692, HIP 17892, HR 1185, Pleiades Sector IR-W d1-55, and Pleiades Sector KC-V c2-4. Professor Alba Tesreau hopes observing Thargoid activities outside combat zones will increase understanding of their behaviour.
3304-03-10
Week in Review
This week's major stories include the discovery of new Guardian sites, with engineer Ram Tah producing blueprints for new Guardian-based weaponry and power-plant systems. Federal President Zachary Hudson and Imperial Admiral Denton Patreus welcomed the new armaments. Aegis launched the Eagle Eye initiative with orbital surveillance installations at six locations in the Pleiades Nebula to monitor Thargoid activity. The Coalition of Othime announced plans to build a new asteroid base for refinery expansion.
3304-03-08
The Guardians The Galaxy Reacts
Reactions to the discovery of new Guardian sites and Ram Tah's Guardian-inspired technologies have been varied. Federal President Zachary Hudson and Imperial Admiral Denton Patreus welcomed the new armaments for anti-Thargoid defence. Alliance Prime Minister Edmund Mahon praised the expansion of scientific knowledge. Simguru Pranav Antal of Utopia looked forward to shared prosperity. Professor Alba Tesreau of Aegis expressed hope that studying Guardian data would enhance understanding of Thargoid motives.
3304-03-04
Week in Review
This week's major stories include the discovery of a new Thargoid Scout Marauder vessel in the Pleiades and core system fringes, reported to be more manoeuvrable but less resistant to weapons fire than Interceptors. Aegis confirmed Thargoids are specifically targeting starports with Aegis laboratories, and defensive measures have been increased. Colonia authorities decided to build a new security installation, while Arek Crimson Vision Corporation launched a weapons campaign for Colonia. Admiral Denton Patreus announced the successful conclusion of a joint Federal-Imperial operation in the Pleiades Sector OI-T c3-7 and Arietis Sector XE-Z b4 systems.
3304-02-16
Proactive or Reactive
As Thargoid attacks continue in the Pleiades, Imperial Senator Gianna Tachibana has criticized the lack of an effective military response. She told the Imperial Herald that independent pilot militias and Aegis research have had impact, but humanity is being reactive rather than proactive. She called for a comprehensive military strategy and coordinated response.
3304-02-16
The Bulwark Project
The Federation has launched the Bulwark Project, an initiative to develop autonomous military hardware to fight the Thargoids. Federal Shadow President Felicia Winters stated the project aims to minimize casualties while continuing defense, dismissing Imperial concerns about AI development by clarifying it is not intended to develop machine intelligence.
3304-02-09
Galactic Leaders Renew Appeals for Aid
Seventeen starports have now been attacked by the Thargoids, leaving widespread destruction and many stations in critical condition. Federal President Zachary Hudson and Emperor Arissa Lavigny-Duval have issued fresh appeals for aid. The Emperor also confirmed that Aegis is investigating reports that the Thargoids are moving toward the core systems.
3304-01-13
New Technology from Aegis
Aegis has released the decontamination limpet, a new device designed to counter the caustic properties of Thargoid enzyme missiles. The device strips corrosive enzymes from a ship's hull while performing limited repairs. Aegis claims it has been rigorously field tested and will give pilots a distinct defensive advantage.
3304-01-12
One Year On
One year after the Thargoids returned to human-occupied space, their aggression has escalated to starport attacks in the Pleiades Nebula. Political commentator Daxton Sung criticizes the superpowers' responses, noting militaristic rhetoric from Hudson, noncommittal statements from Lavigny-Duval, and sympathetic words from Mahon, while questioning Aegis's weapons program as the only solution.
