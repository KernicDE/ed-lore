3301-09-03
The Hutton Mug
BlipMagnet announced the Hutton Mug, a commemorative beaker for pilots who complete the epic journey to Hutton Orbital, located 0.22 light years from the nearest star in Alpha Centauri. The company appealed for scrap materials to produce the mugs, offering participants their own Hutton mug as reward. The mug is supposedly made from salvaged Frame Shift Drive plates of ships that ran out of fuel en route.
