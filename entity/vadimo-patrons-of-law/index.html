3304-04-27
The Biggest Party of 3304
The Vadimo Patrons of Law, an Imperial organisation based in Vadimo, has announced plans to host an elaborate soirée. A spokesperson stated they want to give the people of Vadimo a chance to forget their worries amid the Thargoid threat. They have placed an open order for commodities and promised rewards to pilots who deliver goods to Sikorsky Station, along with kill orders on wanted ships to protect traders.
