Alien 'Barnacles' Discovered in the Pleiades
Dr Arcanonn of the Canonn Interstellar Research Group confirmed the discovery of alien 'barnacle' structures on at least two planets in the Pleiades Nebula. Commander Octo discovered the first barnacles on Merope 5c, with additional sites found on Pleiades Sector JC-U B3-2. The structures produce meta-alloys, emit sounds similar to Unknown Artefacts, bear mysterious markings, and cause ship malfunctions. Crashed ships have been found near many barnacles.
